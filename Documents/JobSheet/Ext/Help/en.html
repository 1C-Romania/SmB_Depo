Job sheet document

The document registers the fact that some job has been completed by some employee in order to calculate employee's pay sheet.

 

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Structural unit - responsible for transaction, selected from the Structural unit list.
  • Assignee - of the document. It is selected from the Performers or Crew lists. IN case when workgroup is selected as a performer, members of workgroup and LPF can be specified on the tab Workgroup Roster.
  • Currency - of the document, selected from the Currencies list.
  • Closed, Closing Company contract date - in case when this flag is checked, salary will be calculated on the Closing Company contract date.
  • Note - any additional information.
  • Operations. The table contains planned and completed operations with the option to specify a customer order and a product. Table fields:
    • Company contract date - when operation was completed.
    • Customer order - of the operation, selected from the list of Customer Order documents.

    • ProductsAndServices - for the production of which the operation has been executed. It is selected from the ProductsAndServices list.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the ProductsAndServices Characteristics list. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches list. The attribute is displayed on the form if batches are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • UOM - unit of measure. It is selected from the UOM Classifier list or from the Units of Measure list.
    • Operation - selected from the ProductsAndServices list.
  • Quantity (Plan) - planned COUNT of the items to be produced in units of measure selected in current row.
  • Quantity (fact) - actual COUNT of produced items in units of measure selected in current row.
  • Time Norm - time required to manufacture one unit of the item.
  • Tariff - cost of operation of production of one unit of the item.
  • Standard Hours - COUNT of standard hours.
  • Cost - of the operations performed.
  • Workgroup Roster. The table contains list of employees that are the members of the workgroup executed the order. Table attributes:
    • Performer - of the workgroup, selected from the Performers list.
    • LPF - coefficient of labor participation in proportion to which cost of the work is divided. Part but not percentage is specified in this field.
