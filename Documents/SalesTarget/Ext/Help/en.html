Sales target document

The document stores information on target sales. Sales are planned by total and quantitative indicators.

Document attributes:

  • Organization - an internal organization for which the document is created. It is selected from the Companies catalog. The attribute is displayed in the form if accounting by multiple organizations is enabled in the accounting options.

  • Structural unit for which the sales plan is created. It is selected from the Structural units catalog, the type is "Administrative managerial division". The attribute is optional.

  • Planning period which defines the periodicity, start and end date of the scheduled sales period. It is selected from the Planning periods catalog.

To change the price kinds, discounts and currencies, click Edit prices and currencies in the document form.

  • Inventory. The table can be filled in using selection (click Select in the command bar of the tabular section) or manual data input. The tabular section includes:

    • Products and services planned to be sold. They are selected from the Products and services catalog with the Inventory, Service, or Job item type.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, a characteristic of products and services may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Characteristics of products and services catalog. The attribute is displayed in the form if characteristics accounting is enabled in the accounting parameters.

    • Quantity - a number of planned products and services items.

    • UOM - unit of measure of the target products and services. It is selected from the UOM Classifier catalog or from the Units of measure catalog.

    • Price of the planned products and services item.

    • Amount - cost of products and services to be sold according to this line.

    • VAT rate - value-added tax rate. It is selected from the VAT rates catalog. An optional field.

    • VAT amount - VAT amount for this line.

    • Total - value automatically calculated according to the price and currency settings. To configure settings, click Edit prices and currency.

    • Customer order - the requirements source; it is selected as a Customer order document. An optional field.

    • Planning date - planning period start date. By default, it is equal to the predefined start date of the planning period.

  • Additional information. A group of attributes:

    • Responsible employee - an employee who is responsible for entering the current document; selected from the Performers catalog. An optional field.

    • Comment - a field for any additional information about the document.
