Additional costs document

This document is intended to Save information about the accounting of an increase of the inventories costs received from the counterparties.

Increase in the cost may be caused by the Additional costs, concerning, for example, the transportation costs, security of goods and other services of the counterparties. The inventories can be sent from the one supplier and additional services can be ordered from another one.

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.

  • Structural unit, warehouse - warehouse which is selected from the Structural units list from items of the Structural unit, warehouse type.

  • Work order -  Work order document. The attribute is optional.

  • Counterparty - vendor selected from the Counterparties list.

  • Company contract - agreement between company and company. It is selected from the list of Counterparty Company contracts.

To edit document currency, click on the Edit Prices and Currency button.

  • Inventory. Document table can be filled using pick method (button Pick on the command bar), or data can be entered manually. Table fields:

    • Receipt Document - reference to the  Supplier invoice document, containing purchase with Additional costs.

    • ProductsAndServices - item selected from the ProductsAndServices list with the Inventory item type.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the ProductsAndServices Charatcteristics list. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches list. The attribute is displayed on the form if batches are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Quantity - COUNT of items received.

    • UOM - unit of measure. It is selected from the UOM Classifier list or from the Units of Measure list.

    • Price - price of item received.

    • Amount - cost of item received.

    • VAT rate - VAT rate selected from the VAT rates list.

    • Including VAT - Amount of VAT.

    • Total - total Amount calculated according to the price and currency settings. To change the settings click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

    • Customer Order - Customer Order document.

    • Amount of expense - cost of expenses distributed on the cost the items received. The cost of expenses can be distributed via the command bar button Distribute.

  • Expenses. Document table can be filled using pick method Pick on the command bar), or data can be entered manually. Table fields:

    • ProductsAndServices - item selected from the  ProductsAndServices list with the Expense item type.

    • Quantity - COUNT of items.

    • UOM - unit of measure. It is selected from the  UOM Classifier list or from the Units of Measure list.

    • Price - item price.

    • Amount - item cost.

    • VAT rate - VAT rate selected from the VAT rates list.

    • Including VAT - Amount of VAT.

    • Total - total Amount calculated according to the price and currency settings. To change the settings click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

  • Setoff of advance payment. The table contains prepayments received from vendors earlier. To edit data in Prepayment table, click on the Edit Setoff of advance payment button. Table fields:

    • Document - document with vendor prepayment: Expense, Expense from Petty Banking.

      Accounts Amount - Amount in contract currency.

    • Rate - rate used for calculation of the Amount to document currency.

    • Factor - factor used for calculation of the Amount to document currency.

    • Payments Amount - Amount of prepayment in document currency.

  • Additional datarmation. Belongs to group of attributes:

    • Note - any additional information.
