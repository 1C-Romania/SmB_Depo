Fixed asset write-off document

The documents registers write off of fixed assets due to Depreciation or liquidation in case of emergency.

Document operations:

  • Accrual of the balance of depreciation for the month of write off;

  • Record the fixed asset off accounting.

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.

  • Correspondence - GL account where expenses for write off of the fixed asset are accumulated.

  • Note - any additional information.

  • Fixed assets. Table fields:

    • Fixed asset - to be WrittenOff. It is selected from the Fixed assets list.

    • Initial cost - of the fixed asset.

    • Depreciated cost - initial cost less depreciation.

    • Depreciation total - depreciation accrued up until this month.

    • Depreciation This Month - depreciation accrued for the current month.
