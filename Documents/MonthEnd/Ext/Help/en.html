Month end document

The document registers in the database following operations on a monthly basis: direct cost calculation, costing, actual cost calculation, financial performance calculation, calculation of exchange differences, and retail cost calculation (accrual accounting).

 

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.

  • Note - any additional information.
