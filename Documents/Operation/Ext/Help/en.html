Operation document 

The document is used for manual adjustment of records the accounting register.

 

The document contains corrective accounting records affecting accounts of the Headial register. IN addition, the document can be used to registration of any custom transactions.

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.

  • Note - any additional information.

  • Accounting Records. Table attributes:

    • Account Dr - of the accounting record, selected from the Managerial chart of accounts.

    • Currency Debit - of the accounting record. The attribute is available if selected debit account is marked as currency account. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.

    • Amount (Cur.) Dr - Amount in currency of debit account. The attribute is available if selected debit account is marked as currency account. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.

    • Account Cr - of the accounting record, selected from the Managerial chart of accounts.

    • Currency Credit - of the accounting record. The attribute is available if selected debit account is marked as currency account. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.

    • Amount (Cur.) Cr - Amount in currency of credit account. The attribute is available if selected credit account is marked as currency account. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.

    • Amount - of the accounting record.

    • Content - custom text description.
