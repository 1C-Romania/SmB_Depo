Work order document

The document is designed to register the buyer’s intention to order works, services, planning of company resource workload.

The list of works, services that are ordered is Statusd in the tabular section Work.

  • Works. tabular section is available only for operation type “Order-request”. This tabular section may be filled by selection (button "Pick" of the command bar of tabular section) or may be entered manually. Attributes of the table:

  • Type of works - specifies the cost of the standard hour.
  • Work, service - the performed work, the provided service.

  • Characteristics - if necessary in this field indicates the characteristics works, services. It is selected from the catalog "Characteristics of the item". Attribute is displayed on the form if the accounting on item characteristics is enable in the parameters settings.

  • Time allowance - is norm of work performance. For works on a fixed price fixed time is equal to unit.

  • Multiplicity - is the Amount of works the norm of which is indicated.

  • Factor - is the Amount of performed work, provided service.

  • Price - is the price of work, service.

  • % Discount - is discount percent (markup).

  • Amount - is the cost for the row.

  • VAT Rate - is a value added tax. It is selected from the catalog "VAT rates".

  • Including VAT - is sum on value added tax on given line.

  • Total - is the field are automatically filled the total value of works and services according to the setting of prices and exchange rates. Settings are set in the dialog, called by the button "Edit prices and currency".

  • Specification. Defines the variant of work execution. According to the specification, you can automatically fill in the used materials.

  • Content - it is the presentation of work in print.

To make visible the requisites of the tabular section Work - Type of works, Characteristics, Multiplicity, Factor, % Discount, Amount, VAT Rate, Including VAT, Total, Specification, Content it’s necessary on the command bar of this part of table to execute command "All actions" - "Change form" and set the flag of attribute visible.

In the tabular section Engaged resources is indicated the list of company resources, which loading should be planned.

The bar Resources schedule is intended to analyze the loading and quick select of resources to the tabular section Engaged resources.

The following attributes should be filled in the document:

  • Company. IN this attribute the own company, issued the document is indicated. It is selected from the catalog "Companies". Attribute is indicated in the form if in the parameters account settings is enabled accounting for several companies.
  • Counterparty. IN this attribute the information about contractor-customer is indicated. It is selected from the catalog "Counterparties".

  • Company contract. IN this attribute is indicated the contract of contractor in which the document is issued. It is selected from the catalog "Company Company contracts".

On the form of the document there is the button "Edit prices and currency", which opens a dialog to edit the kinds of prices, discounts, and currency of the document.
