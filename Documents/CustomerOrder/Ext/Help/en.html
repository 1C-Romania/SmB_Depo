Customer order document

The document is used for registration of customer requests for purchase of goods, jobs and services.

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Transaction kind - one of the following: Sales Order or Processing Order.
  • Counterparty - customer selected from the Counterparty list.

  • Company contract - agreement between company and company. It is selected from the list of Counterparty Company contracts.

  • Project - selected from the Projects list.

To change the price types, discounts and currencies, click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

  • Inventory. Document table can be filled using pick method (button Pick on the command bar), or data can be entered manually. Table fields:

    • Item - item being ordered.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics list. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Quantity - COUNT of items.

    • Reserve - COUNT of items to be reserved.

    • UOM - unit of measure. It is selected from the UOM Classifier list or from the Units of Measure list.

    • Price - item price.

    • Discount, % - discount (markup) rate.

    • Amount - cost of ordered item.

    • VAT rate - VAT rate selected from the VAT rates list.

    • Including VAT - Amount of VAT.

    • Total - total Amount calculated according to the price and currency settings. To change the settings click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

    • Specification - specification which defines a set of rules for production of the ordered item.
    • Content - text of order in the print form. To enable the attribute go to All actions - Change form... and check the flag.

  • Jobs - the table is available only for the transaction type "Job order. Document table can be filled using pick method (button Pick on the command bar), or data can be entered manually. Table fields:

    • Job Type - item selected from the ProductsAndServices list with the Job Type item type.
    • Job, tools - service /job provided to customer.
    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics list. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.
    • Time Norm - standard time required to complete the job. The norm equals 1 for the jobs having fix cost.

    • Factor - COUNT of completed jobs / services provided.

    • Price - price of job / service.

    • Discount, % - discount (markup) rate.

    • Amount - cost of job / service.

    • VAT rate - VAT rate selected from the VAT rates list.

    • Including VAT - Amount of VAT.

    • Total Amount - total Amount calculated according to the price and currency settings. To change the settings click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

    • Specification - selected from the Specifications list. Materials, required for the job, will be automatically filled according to selected specification.

    • Materials - materials, required for the job.

    • Performers - employees who perform the job (with indication of the tariff).

    • Content - text of job in the print form. To enable the attribute go to All actions - Change form... and check the flag.

    • The document Customer Order can be entered on the basis of the Counterparty list.
