Work order document 

The document is used for planning employee work schedule.

 

Document transaction types:

  • external - job according to specific Order, Company contract, for some Counterparty.

  • internal - job for the company.

    • Document attributes:

    • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
    • Transaction kind - internal or external.
    • Structural units - where the task is planned. It is selected from the Structural units list.
    • Performer - to whom the task is assigned. Selected from the Performers list.
    • Key Resource - used for execution of the task. Selected from the Key resources list.
    • Consumer - for whom the task is planned. Selected from one of the lists: Counterparties, Counterparty Company contracts, Customer order.
    • Job (tools) - being produced, selected from the ProductsAndServices list.
    • Characteristic - of job (service), selected from the ProductsAndServices Characteristics list.
    • Job Type - by which activity is scheduled. It is selected from the ProductsAndServices list.
    • Company contract date - projected date of completion of the task.
    • From - task start time.
    • To - task end time.
    • Duration - task duration, in HH:MM.
    • Duration (H) - task duration, hours.
    • Price - cost of 1 hour of work.
    • Amount - cost of the task for a client.
    • Closed - if flag is checked the task will not be displayed on desktop in the list of scheduled tasks.
    • Abbreviation - any additional information.

    The Time tracking document can be Creatingd on the basis of the Job Order document.
