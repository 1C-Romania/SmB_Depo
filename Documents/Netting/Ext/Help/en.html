Debt adjustment document

Document is used for registration of mutual settlements between companies.

Document transaction types:

  • Debt adjustment;
  • Customer debt assignment;
  • Debt assignment to vendor;
  • Customer debt adjustment;
  • Vendor debt adjustment.

Mutual settlement operation is used for registration of a mutual settlement with a company.

If customer debt assignment should be registered then the Customer debt assignment must be selected.

If debt assignment to vendor should be registered then the Debt assignment to vendor must be selected.

Attributes required for any transaction type:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Note - any additional information.

Attributes required for the Debt adjustment transaction type:

  • Customer - from whom the debt is WrittenOff. Selected from the Counterparties list.
  • Vendor - from whom the company debt is WrittenOff. Selected from the Counerparties list.
  • Accounts receivable. Table fields:
    • Company contract - agreement between company and company. It is selected from the list of Counterparty Company contracts.
    • Document - of the customer debt.
    • Order - specified in the case of payments on specific order. Selected the Customer Order document.
    • Accounts Amount - specified in currency of agreement.
    • Rate - of the currency of agreement. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.
    • Factor - of the rate of agreement currency. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.
    • Accounting Amount - specified in the currency of accounting. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.
  • Accounts Payable. Table attributes:
    • Company contract - agreement between company and company. It is selected from the list of Counterparty Company contracts.
    • Document - of the company debt.
    • Order - specified in the case of payments on specific order. Selected the Work order document.
    • Accounts Amount - specified in currency of agreement.
    • Rate - of the currency of agreement. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.
    • Factor - of the rate of agreement currency. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.
    • Accounting Amount - specified in the currency of accounting. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.

    Attributes required for the Customer debt assignment and the Debt assignment to vendor transaction types located in the Payee attributes table:

    • Company contract - company contract selected from the Counterparty Company contracts list.
    • Document - document of accounts.
    • Order - order document.
    • Accounts Amount - specified in currency of agreement.
    • Rate - of the currency of agreement. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.
    • Factor - of the rate of agreement currency. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.
    • Accounting Amount - specified in the currency of accounting. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.

    Attributes required for the Customer debt assignment transaction type located in document header:

    • Customer - from whom the debt is WrittenOff, selected from the Counterparties list.
    • Customer Recipient - to whom the debt is transferred, selected from the Counterparties list.

    Attributes required for the Debt assignment to vendor transaction type located in document header:

    • Vendor - from whom company's debt is WrittenOff, selected from the Counterparties list.
    • Vendor Recipient - to whom company's debt is transferred, selected from the Counterparties list.

    Attributes required for the Customer debt adjustment transaction type located in document header:

    • Account - GL account.

    Attributes required for the Vendor debt adjustment transaction type located in document header:

    • Account - GL account.
