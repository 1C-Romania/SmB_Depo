Enter opening balance document 

The document registers all the opening balances in the database.

 

The balances entering is performed by different sections of accounting. A section is selected in the Accounting section attribute.

 

To input data without special accounting section, select the Other Sections option.

Depending on the selected section, the data content and the document form will be changed.

After data input is completed it is recommended to verify the balances by generating the report of opening balances.
