Payroll sheet document

The document allows you to generate a payroll sheet print form to pay salary to the company's employees.

Depending on the transaction kind, the following payroll sheets can be generated using the document:

  • Salary
  • Advance

Document attributes:

  • Organization - an organization issuing the document. It is selected from the Organizations catalog. The attribute is displayed in the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Transaction kind - document transaction kind: advance or salary.
  • Filter registration period of the payroll sheet.
  • Structural unit for which the payroll sheet is generated.

To change currency of the document and payroll, click Edit prices and currencies.

  • Employees. Table attributes:
    • Employee to whom salary or advance is paid, selected from the Employees catalog.
    • Accrued - an amount to be paid to the employee. The attribute is displayed in the form if currency transactions are enabled in accounting options.
    • For payments - an amount to be paid to the employee. It is specified in the document currency.
