Selection of reserves by result of sales form

A form is intended for picking up into a tabular of the document of the goods which were received on the commission and sold for the period specified on the form of the pickup.

To fill a table of the document, you must:

  • in the field "Quantity" to enter the number of items sold;
  • note in the list those items for which we are reporting to the consignor;
  • using the "Move into document" button the selected information will be added to the tabular section of the document.

     

    Important: IN the tabular section of the pickup form are available only goods in the batches of which there is filled in the field of "Account owner of the batch" and its value corresponds to the set selection.
