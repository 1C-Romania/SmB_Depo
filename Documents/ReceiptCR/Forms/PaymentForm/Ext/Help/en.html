Buyer payment and Receipt CR form

The form appears from the cashier's workplace after you click Accept payment.

You should use different tabs to receive payments in cash and by cards.

On the Cash payment tab, you can enter an amount by clicking number buttons or using the numeric key pad.

There is a drop-down arrow in the amount field. If you click it, a drop-down list appears.

In this list, you can select a document sum or an outstanding balance that has not been paid by cards yet (if an outstanding balance > 0).

You can receive payment by card on the Non-cash payment tab.

If a POS terminal is connected to the application, click Add card to add the card payment.

If a POS terminal is not connected, you can see fields for a single card on the form. If you need to add payment by one more card, click Add card.

To cancel payment by card, click the cancel button to the right from the payment amount.

To enter an amount, click number buttons or hold down ALT and use the numeric key pad.

You can edit an amount for the last added card. If you need to edit an amount for previously added cards, click the calculator button in the relevant field.

There is a drop-down arrow in the amount field. If you click it, a drop-down list appears.

In this list, you can select a document sum or an outstanding balance that has not been paid in cash or by other cards yet (if an outstanding balance > 0).
