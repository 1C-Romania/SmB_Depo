Agent report document

The document Saves information about sales of consignor's stock items by consignee.

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.

  • Counterparty - consignee. It is selected from the list of Counterparties.

  • Company contract - agreement between company and consignee. It is selected from the list of Counterparty Company contracts.

To change the price types and currencies, click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

  • Additionally. Belongs to group of attributes:

    • Brokerage Calculation Method - method of calculation of remuneration. One of the following:

      • Not calculated (remuneration Amount is defined by the parties).

      • Sale percentage.

      • Sale and Cost Net Percentage (sale Amount - consignee sales; cost Amount - cost of items passed to consignee).

    • Commission fee percent - sale percentage that consignee will received for sale of consignor items.

    • VAT brokerage rate - VAT rate that is used for calculation of the Amount of tax applied to brokerage Amount.

    • Do not pay brokerage - brokerage Amount is not paid to the consignee.

  • Customers. IN this tabular section the consignor registrates the information about the customers and disposal dates. When recording the document by lines which are marked as "Invoice is made out" the invoices are generated automatically.

    • Customer - the buyer of the consignor's inventories. Is selected from the "Counterparties" list.

    • Total - the cost of inventories sold by consignor to the customer. The attribute is calculated automatically according to the data of the tabular section of 'Inventory".

    • Invoice is made out - a necessity sign of an automatic invoice registration.

    • Invoice date - the disposal date of the consignor's inventories to the customer.

    • Invoice- link to the invoice made out.

  • Inventory. Document table can be filled using pick method (button Pick on the command bar), or data can be entered manually. Table fields:

    • ProductsAndServices - item sold by consignee. It is selected from the ProductsAndServices list with the Inventory item type.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics list. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches list. The attribute is displayed on the form if batches are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Quantity - COUNT of items sold by consignee.

    • UOM - unit of measure. It is selected from the UOM Classifier list or from the Units of Measure list.

    • Price - price of item sold by consignee.

    • Amount - item total cost.

    • VAT rate - VAT rate selected from the VAT rates

      list.

    • Including VAT - Amount of VAT.

    • Total - total Amount calculated according to the price and currency settings. To change the settings click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

    • Transfer price - item price defined by consignor.

    • Transfer amount - total cost of items being transferred.

    • Transfer Including VAT - Amount of VAT of transferred items.

    • Brokerage amount - brokerage Amount which is calculated based on the consignment agreement (settings on the tab Additionally).

    • Brokerage Including VAT - Amount of VAT of brokerage Amount.

    • Order - Customer Order document. The attribute is optional.

  • Prepayments. The table contains prepayments received from consignee earlier. To edit data in Prepayment table, click on the Edit Setoff of advance payment button. Table fields:

    • Document - document with consignee prepayment: Petty Banking Receipt, Payment receipt.

    • Order - reference to the document Customer Order.

    • Amount of settlements - Amount in contract currency.

    • Rate - rate used for calculation of the Amount to document currency.

    • Factor - factor used for calculation of the Amount to document currency.

    • Payments Amount - Amount of prepayment in document currency.

  • Additional datarmation. Belongs to group of attributes:

    • Structural unit - division which is selected from the StructuralUnits/Help">Structural units list from items of the Structural unit type. The attribute is optional.

    • Responsible - information about the employee who enters current document. It is selected from the Performers list. The attribute is optional.

    • Note - any additional information.
