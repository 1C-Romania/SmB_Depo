Inventory pickup, transferred on commission form

The form selects to the tabular section the document of goods which were transferred on commission to the commissionaire and for which he hasn't reported yet.

 

To fill the tabular section of the document, it is necessary:

  • in the field "Quantity" to enter COUNT of the goods sold by the commissionaire;
  • to mark in the list those goods for which the Agent reports;
  • selected information will be added in the tabular section of the document by "Move into Document" button.
