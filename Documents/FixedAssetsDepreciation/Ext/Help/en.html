Fixed asset depreciation document

Document is designed for monthly calculation of exploited property Depreciation and reflection of it in accountancy .

The list of documents is Saved in property accounting documents register.

Depreciation charges start from the first day of a month (if it is evidently not specified to charge in the current month), following the month of the acceptance of assets for the accounting and are charged till the full repayment of the cost of that asset or writing-off of this asset from the accounting.

In the document it is necessary to fill in the following requisites:

  • Company. This requisite indicates organization, for which the calculation and depreciation charges are done. It has to be chosen from the catalogue "Companies". The requisite is displayed in the form if in the settings of accounting parameters IS chosen several organizations for accounting.

  • Note. Specifies the comment to the document.
