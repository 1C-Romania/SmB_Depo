Time tracking document

The document is used for registration of time spent to complete a job.

 

The Time Danagement document can be entered on the basis of the  Job Order document.

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Structural unit - where time is registered, selected from the Structural units list.
  • Performer - who completed a task, selected from the Performers list.
  • Week From ... To ... - week, for which time is registered.
  • Note - any additional information.
  • Operations. The table contains Amount of time worked, with the option to specify Job Type details. The table may be automatically filled on the basis of the existing Job Orders. Table attributes:
    • Consumer - who ordered the job, selected from of the lists: "Companies", "Company Company contracts", "Customer order".
    • Job Type - selected from the ProductsAndServices list.
    • Job (tools) - selected from the ProductsAndServices list.
    • Characteristic - of the job (service), selected from the ProductsAndServices Characteristics list.
    • Total - COUNT of hours, spent to perform the job.
    • Price Type - used for calculation of the cost of the job. It is selected from the Price Kinds list.
    • Tariff - cost per one hour.
    • Amount - total job cost.
    • Duration - of job in hours.
    • From ... To - start and end time of execution of the job.
    • Note - any additional information.
