Cash outflow (Plan) document

The document is used for planning of cash expenses.

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the  Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Currency - selected from the Currencies list.
  • Amount - planned payment Amount in the currency of planning.
  • Item - cash flow item used for planned cash assets expense. It is selected from the Banking flow item list.
  • Payments Group basis - one of the following: approved or not approved.
  • Payments Method - one of the following: non-cash, cash or not defined.
  • Bank account - bank account of an company. The field is available when non-cash payment method is selected. Field value is selected from the Bank accounts.
  • Petty Banking - petty cash of an company. The field is available when cash payment method is selected. Field value is selected from the list of Petty Banking.
  • In. Doc. No - number of incoming document-basis.
  • From - date of incoming document-basis.
  • Document - basis of expense planning.
  • Note - any additional information.
