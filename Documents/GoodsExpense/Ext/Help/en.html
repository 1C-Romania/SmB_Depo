Goods expense document


The document registers expense of inventory items from the order warehouse.

 

The document can be entered on the basis of the financial sales document.

The document reduces COUNT to be deducted from stock (this COUNT was generated by the financial sales document).

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.

  • Basis - document based on which inventory expense is processed.

  • Structural unit, warehouse - from which the inventory will be WrittenOff.

  • Cell - of the warehouse from which the inventory will be WrittenOff.

  • Inventory. Table attributes:

    • ProductsAndServices - selected from the ProductsAndServices list.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the ProductsAndServices Characteristics list. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches list. The attribute is displayed on the form if batches are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Quantity - COUNT of items.

    • UOM - unit of measure. It is selected from the UOM Classifier list or from the Units of Measure list.

  • Note - any additional information.
