1C: Small Business

1C: Smal business is a ready-to-use solution that includes essential management and accounting issues of a small company. Use this configuration to manage such company activities as:

  • Sales, including retail
  • Procurement, including inventory accounting
  • Services
  • Production
  • Monetary assets
  • Payroll and human resources
  • Finance, including property

 

The system is designed only for management accounting. For business and tax accounting, purchase 1C:Enterprise 8.

 

For enterprises comprising of multiple legal entities (organizations), you can maintain management accounting either for all the organizations (for the entire company) or for each organization individually.

Management accounting relies on the data recorded in the electronic documents. An operation fact is entered once and then reflected in different management accounting sections; management balance is also formed.

You can register and generate most primary documentation concerning the company's economic activities: sales, procurement, warehousing, and production. Electronic versions of paper documents provide clear registration of business.

 

Therefore, with 1C:Small Business you can:

  • Manage the enterprise and its executives in charge of business development; analyze, plan and manage your resource flexibly to improve your company's marketability.
  • Provide your team (division leaders, managers, and employees directly handling production, sales, procurement and other activities) with tools for a better effectiveness of their daily work.

1C:Small Business will be the most effective for small companies involved in:

  • Trade
  • Service
  • Contract (project) work
  • Manufacturing
