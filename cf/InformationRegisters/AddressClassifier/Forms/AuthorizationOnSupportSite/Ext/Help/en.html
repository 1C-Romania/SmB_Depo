To download the address classifier, you need to enter your registration data on the 1C:Enterprise user support website:

  • Enter your Login;
  • Enter your Password.
  • If you want your Internet browser to save your login and password for further use, select the Remember password check box.
  • If you are not yet registered, click the Register on user support website hyperlink and complete the registration.
  • Click Continue to proceed to the address classifier download.
