Counterparty products and services information register

The register is intended to store the lists of the suppliers products and services available for delivery.

The register data is used:

  • in the assistant of filling the purchase calendar plan of the Purchase calendar plan data processor to define the available items of the suppliers
  • on the choice form of the products and services catalog when selecting the products and services from the Receipt of goods, services, Purchase orders documents and other receipt documents
  • in the products and services pickup data processor which is run from the merchant documents
