Supplier invoice note document

The document is used to register supplier invoice notes and keep track of numbers of customs cargo declarations.

According to the operation type, you can register an invoice for:

  • Receipt
  • Advance
  • Accrual differences

Fill out the following document attributes:

  • Organization registering the invoice. It is selected from the Organizations catalog. The attribute is displayed in the form if the accounting parameters allow accounting for multiple organizations.
  • Transaction kind of the document: receipt, advance, or accrual differences.
  • Basis. Basis document for the invoice. According to the transaction kind, it can be:  Purchase order, Agent report, Additional costs, Expense report or Supplier invoice.
  • Counterparty. Supplier for which the invoice is issued. It is selected from the Counterparties catalog.
  • Contract of the supplier for which the invoice is issued. It is selected from the Counterparty contracts catalog.
  • Document number. Number of the incoming document.
  • From. Date of the incoming document.
  • Comment. Any additional information regarding the document.

To change the document currency, click Edit prices and currency.

  • Inventory. The tabular section can be filled out by using the Pick method (command bar button Pick of the tabular section) or manual data input. Attributes of the tabular section:

    • Products and services - products and services item specified in the supplier invoice note. It is selected from the Products and services catalog.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, a characteristic of products and services may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics catalog. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for products and services in the accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of products and services may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches catalog. The attribute is displayed in the form if batches are enabled for products and services in accounting options.

    • Quantity - quantity of the products and services item specified in the supplier invoice note.

    • UOM - unit of measure for the line. It is selected from the UOM Classifier or from the Units of Measure list.

    • Price - price of products and services item.

    • Amount - cost of products and services item in the line with taxes according to the check boxes of Edit prices and currency.

    • VAT rate - VAT rate. It is selected from the VAT rates list.

    • VAT amount - amount of VAT for the line.

    • Total - automatically calculated according to the price and currency settings. To change the settings, click Edit prices and currencies.

    • Country of origin of the products and services item. It is selected from the Countries catalog.

    • CCD # - customs cargo declaration number. It is selected from the CCD numbers list.

    • Content - inventory representation in the print form. To enable the attribute, go to All actions - Change form and select the check box.

The Invoice document can be generated based on the following documents:

  • Purchase order
  • Agent report
  • Report to principal
  • Additional costs
  • Expense report
  • Supplier invoice
