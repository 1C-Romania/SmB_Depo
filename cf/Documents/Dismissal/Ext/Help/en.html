Dismissal document

The document registers the event of termination of employment of an employee. The document cancels existing accruals for the dismissed employees.

 

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Note - any additional information.

  • Performers. The table contains reasons and terms of termination of employment. Table attributes:

    • Performer - being dismissed, selected from the Performers list.

    • Dismissal Company contract date - as of which employment is terminated.

    • Reason - for termination of employment.
