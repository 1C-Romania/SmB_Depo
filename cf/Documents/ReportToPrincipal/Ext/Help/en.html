Report to principal document

The document Saves information about reports to consignor for the stock items sold by consignee.

Document attributes:

  • Company - consignee, who received consignors's items for sale. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.

  • Counterparty - consignor. It is selected from the list of Counterparties.

  • Company contract - agreement between company and consignor. It is selected from the list of Counterparty Company contracts.

To change the price types and currencies, click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

  • Additionally. Belongs to group of attributes:

    • Brokerage Calculation Method - method of calculation of remuneration. One of the following:

      • Not calculated (remuneration Amount is defined by the parties).

      • Sale percentage.

      • Sale and Cost Net Percentage (sale Amount - consignee sales; cost Amount - cost of items passed to consignee).

    • Commission fee percent - sale percentage that consignee will received for sale of consignor items.

    • VAT brokerage rate - VAT rate that is used for calculation of the Amount of tax applied to brokerage Amount.

    • Do not pay brokerage - brokerage Amount is not paid to the consignee.

  • Inventory. Document table can be filled using pick method (button Pick on the command bar), or data can be entered manually. Table fields:

    • ProductsAndServices - item sold by the company. It is selected from the ProductsAndServices list with the Inventory item type.

      Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics list. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches list. The attribute is displayed on the form if batches are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Quantity - COUNT of items sold by the company.

    • UOM - unit of measure. It is selected from the UOM Classifier list or from the Units of Measure list.

    • Price - price of item sold by the company.

    • Amount - item total cost.

    • VAT rate - VAT rate selected from the VAT rates list.

    • Including VAT - Amount of VAT.

    • Total - total Amount calculated according to the price and currency settings. To change the settings click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

    • Receipt Price - item price defined by consignor.

    • Receipt Amount - total cost of items being received.

    • Receipt Including VAT - Amount of VAT of received items.

    • Brokerage amount - brokerage Amount which is calculated based on the consignment agreement (settings on the tab Additionally).

    • Brokerage Including VAT - Amount of VAT of brokerage Amount.

    • Work order - Purchase order document. The attribute is optional.

    • Customer Order - Customer order document. The attribute is optional.

  • Prepayments. The table contains prepayments paid to consignor earlier. To edit data in Prepayment table, click on the Edit Setoff of advance payment button. Table fields:

    • Document - document with payment to consignor: Expense from Petty Banking, Expense.

    • Order - reference to the document Purchase order.

    • Amount of settlements - Amount in contract currency.

    • Rate - rate used for calculation of the Amount into document currency.

    • Factor - factor used for calculation of the Amount into document currency.

    • Payments Amount - Amount of prepayment in document currency.

  • Additional datarmation. Belongs to group of attributes:

    • Structural unit - division which is selected from the StructuralUnits/Help">Structural units list from items of the Structural unit type. The attribute is optional.

    • Note - any additional information.
