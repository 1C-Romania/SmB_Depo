Inventory reservation document

The document is designed to record inventory reservation for storage and production and to specify requirements of customer orders in purchase orders and assembly/production orders.

Fill out the following document attributes:

  • Organization - an organization for which the document is created. It is selected from the Organizations catalog. The field is shown in the form if the accounting parameters allow accounting for multiple organizations.
  • Customer order - a document to which inventory items are reserved.
  • Inventory. Tabular section:
    • Products and services - selected from catalog of products and services.
    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the products and services item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics catalog. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for products and services in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of the products and services item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Batches catalog. The attribute is displayed on the form if batches are enabled for the products and services items in accounting options.

    • UOM - unit of measure.
    • Quantity - quantity of items of products and services.
    • Original reserve place - an empty value is specified if items are reserved. Structural unit (warehouse or production) or order (assembly order, production order or purchase order) is selected to cancel reservation.
    • New reserve place - an empty value is specified if reservation has to be canceled. Structural unit or order is selected to reserve the items.
  • Comment - any additional information.
