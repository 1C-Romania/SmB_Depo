Processing report document

The document Saves the information about processing of the raw materials received from customers.

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.

  • Customer Order - according to which the inventory has been accepted for processing. It is selected the Customer order list. The attribute is optional.

  • Structural unit, warehouse - department from which the finished product is dispatched. It is selected from the StructuralUnits/Help">Structural units list of the Structural unit, warehouse type.

  • Cell - of the department (rack, sector) from which the product is dispatched. It is selected from the Structural unit, warehouse Cells list. It is selected from the Structural unit, warehouse Cells list. The attribute is displayed on the form if warehouses having cells are enabled in the accounting options.

  • Counterparty - who provides raw materials for processing, selected from the Counterparties list.

  • Company contract - agreement between company and company. It is selected from the list of Counterparty Company contracts.

To change the price types, discounts and currencies, click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

  • Inventory. The table can be filled using pick method (button Pick on the command bar), or data can be entered manually. Table fields:

    • Nomencalture - that is produced after processing of raw materials. It is selected from the ProductsAndServices of the Inventory type.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics list. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches list. The attribute is displayed on the form if batches are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Quantity - of produced items.

    • Reserve - of reserved items.

    • UOM - unit of measure. It is selected from the UOM Classifier list or from the Units of Measure list.

    • Price - of produced item.

    • Discount, % - discount (markup) rate.

    • Amount - item cost.

    • VAT rate - selected from the VAT rates list.

    • Including VAT - Amount of VAT.

    • Total - total Amount calculated according to the price and currency settings. To change the settings click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

    • Specification - containing information about the production technology. It is selected from the Specifications list. The attribute is optional.

    • Content - item presentation in the print form. To enable the attribute it is required to check the flag in the section All actions - Form change.

  • Materials. The table can be filled using pick method (button Pick on the command bar), or data can be entered manually. Table fields:

    • ProductsAndServices - material costs attributed to production of finished goods. It is selected from the ProductsAndServices of the Inventory type.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics list. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches list. The attribute is displayed on the form if batches are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Quantity - of items attributed to production of finished goods.

    • UOM - unit of measure. It is selected from the UOM Classifier list or from the Units of Measure list.

    • Price - of material costs (customer's items).

    • Amount - item cost.

    • VAT rate - selected from the VAT rates list.

    • Including VAT - Amount of VAT.

    • Total - total Amount calculated according to the price and currency settings. To change the settings click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

  • Disposals. The table can be filled using pick method (button Pick on the command bar), or data can be entered manually. Table fields:

    • ProductsAndServices - to be disposed, selected from the ProductsAndServices list of the Inventory type.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics list. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches list. The attribute is displayed on the form if batches are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Quantity - of items to be disposed.

    • UOM - unit of measure. It is selected from the UOM Classifier list or from the Units of Measure list.

  • Prepayments. The table contains prepayments received from customers earlier. To edit data in Prepayment table, click on the Edit Setoff of advance payment button. Table fields:

    • Document - document with customer prepayment: Petty Banking Receipt, Payment receipt.

    • Amount of settlements - Amount in contract currency.

    • Rate - rate used to recalculate Amount to document currency.

    • Factor - factor used to recalculate Amount to document currency.

    • Payments Amount - Amount of prepayment in document currency.

  • Additional datarmation. Belongs to group of attributes:

    • Structural unit - which is responsible for the operation. It is selected from the StructuralUnits/Help">Structural units list of the Structural unit type. The attribute is optional.

    • Responsible - information about the employee who enters current document. It is selected from the Performers list. The attribute is optional.

    • Note - any additional information.
