Other expenses document

The document registers other (non-material) costs (expenses) in the database.

 

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Structural unit - warehouse or production department for which the expenses are registered.
  • Account - corr. account with the 'Other income' or 'Other Current Assets' type.
  • Expenses. Table attributes:
    • GL Expense Account - GL account, where the expenses are attributed.
    • Business Activity - to which expenses are related.
    • Customer Order - to which the expenses are attributed.
    • Amount - of expenses in currency of accounting.
  • Note - any additional information.
