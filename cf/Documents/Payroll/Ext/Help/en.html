Payroll document

The document is used for calculation a payroll and processing of deductions.

The document can be Creatingd manually, or it can be filled automatically via Fill and Calculate buttons located on the command bar of the form.

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Note - any additional information.
  • Filter registration period - month of payroll calculation.
  • Structural unit - destination division selected from the Structural units list.
  • Currency - currency of accrual. The document is filled only by those planned accruals that have currency equal to document currency. It means that new document should be Creatingd for each payroll currency. It is selected from the Currencies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.

  • Accruals and Deductions. The table contains salaries calculated for the employees and the way the payroll will be attributed to expenses. Document attributes:

    • Performer - employee whose salary is calculated, selected from the Performers list.
    • Position - of the employee. It is selected from the Positions list.
    • Accrual kind (deduction) - accrual or deduction, selected from the Accrual and deduction types list.

    • Beg Company contract date, End Company contract date - period for which the salary is calculated.

    • Days - number of days that the employee has worked on the specified position with specified parameters of salary calculation (filled in automatically in time sheet).

    • Hours - number of hours during which the employee has worked on the specified position with specified parameters of salary calculation (filled in automatically in time sheet).

    • Rate - of planned accrual or deduction in the document currency.

    • Amount - calculated Amount of accrual or deduction.

    • GL Expense Account - GL account of expenses where accrual or deduction will be attributed.

    • Business Activity - where accrual or deduction will be attributed, selected from the Business Activities list.

    • Customer Order - where accrual or deduction will be attributed, selected from the Customer order list.

    • K - flag indicating that row has been modified manually and that it has to be excluded from calculation. The Amount will not be refilled.

    • Indicator 1, Indicator 3, Indicator 3 - calculation parameters, whose values are required for calculation using the formula of accrual type.

    • Value 1, Value 3, Value 3 - values of the indicators. They are calculated during auto filling.

  • Belongs to group income taxes. The table contains data about accrued income taxes. The table is displayed on the form if income tax accounting is enabled in accounting options.

    • Performer - to whom the tax is accrued, selected from the Performers list.
    • Accrual kind (deduction) - tax. Selected from the Accrual and deduction types list.
    • Amount - tax Amount.
