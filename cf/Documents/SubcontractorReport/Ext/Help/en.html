Subcontractor report document 

The document Saves the information about raw materials processed by a subcontractor.

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.

  • Basis - the Purchase order document, used to generate current document. The attribute is optional.

  • Customer order - selected from the Customer order list. The attribute is optional.

  • Structural unit - where result of the processing is attributed. It is selected from the Structural units list from items of the Structural unit, warehouse or tools type.

  • Cell - of the division (rack, sector) where result of the processing is attributed. It is selected from the Structural unit, warehouse Cells list. The attribute is displayed on the form if warehouses having cells are enabled in the accounting options.

  • Counterparty - subcontractor, selected from the Counterparties list.

  • Company contract - agreement between company and subcontractor. It is selected from the list of Counerparty Company contracts.

To change the price types, discounts and currencies, click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

  • Finished goods. Belongs to group of attributes:

    • ProductsAndServices - that is produced after processing of raw materials by a subcontractor. It is selected from the ProductsAndServices of the Inventory type.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics list. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches list. The attribute is displayed on the form if batches are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Quantity - of produced items.

    • UOM - unit of measure. It is selected from the UOM Classifier list or from the Units of Measure list.

    • Specification - containing information about the production technology. It is selected from the Specifications list. The attribute is optional.

  • Expenses. Belongs to group of attributes:

    • ProductsAndServices - additional expense, appeared as a result of processing. It is selected from the ProductsAndServices of the Expense type.

    • Amount - cost of expenses.

    • VAT rate - selected from the VAT rates list.

    • Including VAT - Amount of VAT.

    • Total - total Amount calculated according to the price and currency settings. To change the settings click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

  • Inventory. The table can be filled using pick method (button Pick on the command bar), or data can be entered manually. Table fields:

    • ProductsAndServices - raw material that was processed by subcontractor. It is selected from the ProductsAndServices of the Inventory type.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics list. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches list. The attribute is displayed on the form if batches are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Quantity - of items used by subcontractor.

    • UOM - unit of measure. It is selected from the UOM Classifier list or from the Units of Measure list.

    • Specification - containing information about the production technology. It is selected from the Specifications list. The attribute is optional.

    • Price - of used item.

    • Amount - item cost.

    • VAT rate - selected from the VAT rates list.

    • Including VAT - Amount of VAT.

    • Total - total Amount calculated according to the price and currency settings. To change the settings click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

  • Disposals. The table can be filled using pick method (button Pick on the command bar), or data can be entered manually. Table fields:

    • ProductsAndServices- to be disposed, selected from the ProductsAndServices list of the Inventory type.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics list. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches list. The attribute is displayed on the form if batches are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Quantity - of items to be disposed.

    • UOM - unit of measure. It is selected from the UOM Classifier list or from the Units of Measure list.

  • Prepayments. The table contains prepayments received from customers earlier. To edit data in Prepayment table, click on the Edit Setoff of advance payment button. Table fields:

    • Document - document with vendor prepayment: Expense, Expense from Petty Banking.

      Amount of settlements - Amount in contract currency.

    • Rate - rate used for calculation of the Amount to document currency.

    • Factor - factor used for calculation of the Amount to document currency.

    • Payments Amount - Amount of prepayment in document currency.

  • Additional datarmation. Belongs to group of attributes:

    • Note - any additional information.
