Transfer between cells document

The document moves inventory items between warehouse cells.

Document attributes:

  • Organization - an internal organization, for which the document is created.
  • Transaction kind - one of the following: From several cells to one or From one cell to several.
  • Warehouse - it is selected from the Structural units catalog: the warehouse or structural unit housing the inventory movements.
  • Cell - a cell of the warehouse from which the inventory will be written off in case of transaction From one to several or received in case of transaction From several to one.
  • Inventory. Table fields:
    • Products and services - selected from the relevant catalog.
    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of products and services may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics catalog. The attribute is displayed in the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of products and services may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches catalog. The attribute is displayed in the form if batches are enabled for products and services in accounting options.

    • UOM - unit of measure of items of products and services.
    • Quantity - quantity of products and services.
    • Cell -  a cell of the warehouse from which the inventory will be written off in case of transaction From several to one or received in case of transaction From one to several.
  • Comment - any additional information.
