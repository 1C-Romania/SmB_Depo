Settlements reconciliation document

The document is designed for control and registration of settlements reconciliation. Settlements reconciliation parties are a company of trading enterprise and a counterparty (a legal entity or an individual of the partner). Settlements reconciliation takes several steps. For each step, a certain status of the reconciliation document is registered: created, on reconciliation, checked.

  • Created. Settlements reconciliation is created. Document may be created manually or using reconciliation creation assistant (the batch document entry). The document is printed and sent to a counterparty.
  • On reconciliation. Document is sent to a counterparty. The counterparty compares company’s data with its data. Information about discrepancies on settlement debts is filled out on the Data from counterparty page.
  • Checked. Parties of settlements reconciliation agreed on the mutual claims of settlements and signed the document.

The document could be issued on the counterparty initiative. IN this case, a new document is created, where according to the print form of the reconciliation act, information about the counterparty debt is registered. Status "On reconciliation" is set for the document. Using the reports, you can compare information about debt according to counterparty data and our data. You can also print detailed information specifying a list of documents on which the debt occurred, according to our and counterparty data. For these purposes, Reconciliation act of mutual settlements (with debt according to counterparty data) is used. IN the print form, you can open and edit any document with debt.

Important. When you fill out tabular section Company details, specify the Finish attribute value as a document date for documents of the Work order kind. This value will be used to populate attributes Document date and Document description. You can view the original value of the Work order document date in the Settlement document field.
