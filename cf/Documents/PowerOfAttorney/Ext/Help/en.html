Power of attorney document

The document is intended to be used to create a power of attorney for receiving products.

Information about an authorized representative is selected from the Individuals catalog.

Fill out the following document attributes:

  • Organization - an internal organization issuing the document. It is selected from the Organizations catalog. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.

  • Basis - a basis document for issuing the power of attorney. The Purchase order document is selected.

  • Authorized person - to whom the power of attorney is issued, selected from the Individuals catalog.

  • Valid till - date until the power of attorney is valid.

  • Bank account - company bank account number. The attribute is displayed in print forms. It is selected from the Bank accounts catalog.

  • Counterparty - for whom the power of attorney is issued, it is selected from the Counterparties catalog.

  • Contract - company contract selected from the Counterparty contracts catalog.

  • For receipt from - a full name of the company. The attribute is displayed in the print form.

  • By document - a full name of the basis document. The attribute is displayed in the print form.

  • Comment - any additional information.

  • Inventory. Tablular section:

    • Inventory abbreviation - item to be received using power or attorney.

    • UOM - unit of measure.

    • Quantity - quantity of items to be received using a power of attorney.
