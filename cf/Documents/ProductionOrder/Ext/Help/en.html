Production order document

Production order is a document ordering a set of goods from the warehouse or ordering a production division to manufacture some products, perform work, or provide services.

This document forms a scope/calendar plan for a delivery, production, work, or services.

In the document header, specify the organizational unit - a warehouse or a production division - and the order start/finish dates.

In the Products table area, enter the goods and services to produce (the Assembly operation kind) or disassemble (the Disassembly operation kind).

The Materials table area specifies materials and semi-products required to manufacture the product (the Assembly operation kind), or states the goods and services, i.e. the result of disassembly (the Disassembly operation kind).

The Applied resources table area specifies a list of the enterprise resources which load needs to be planned.

If you use inventory reservation mechanism, you can open the order linked to a customer order; thus, you will direct the requirements of the customer order to the goods to produce under your order. The necessary materials can also be reserved at the specified warehouse for the customer order.

Use the Settings - Accounting settings section to configure the mechanisms of inventory reservation, process operation and resource planning.

Production order document can be added on the basis of:

  • Customer order
  • Production order
