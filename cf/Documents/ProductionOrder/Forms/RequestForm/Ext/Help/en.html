Production order document

The document represents the order to a warehouse on goods assembling or to production division on production, work execution, services rendering.

This document actually Creatings the volume-calendar schedule of assembling, inventory production, work execution, services rendering.

Company - a warehouse or production division, and also the date and time of start making order (start) and time and date ending performing order (finish) are specified in the document header.

 The item which needs to be made is specified in tabular section tools.

The list of the enterprise resources which loading must be planned is specified in the Resources in use tabular section.

The Resources Schedule panel is intended for the loading analysis and fast pickup of resources in the Resources in use tabular section.
