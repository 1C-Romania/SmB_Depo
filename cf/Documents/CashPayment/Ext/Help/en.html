Cash payment document 

The document registers transactions of outgoing cash assets in the database.

 

Document transaction types:

  • To Vendor.
  • To Customer.
  • To Advance Holder.
  • Salary of Statusment.
  • Salary to employee 
  • Taxes.
  • Transfer to the cash register
  • Other.

The To Vendor transaction is used for processing of the cash payments to vendors.

If it is required to make a payment to a customer for the previously returned items, then the To Customer transaction is selected.

If it is required to issue funds to an advance holder - the To Advance Holder transaction is selected.

If it is required to process a payroll - the Salary of Statusment transaction is selected.

If it is required to process a payment for a specific employee - the Salary to employee transaction is selected.

When transferring the cash assets to the cash register use the Transfer to the cash register transaction type.

In case of tax payments - the Taxes transaction is selected.

In other cases - the Other transaction is selected.

Attributes required for any transaction type:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Transaction kind - of the document.
  • Basis - document of the payment.
  • Petty Banking - of the Company, for which payment is processed. It is selected from the Petty Bankinges list.
  • Item - of cash flow, selected from the Banking flow item list.
  • Amount - of payment in currency of the petty cash.
  • Currency - of the petty cash, selected from the Currencies list.
  • Issue - displayed in the corresponding field of the print form.
  • Payments Basis - displayed in the corresponding field of the print form.
  • Appendix - displayed in the corresponding field of the print form.
  • By - displayed in the corresponding field of the print form.
  • Note - any additional information.

Attributes required for To Vendor or To Customer transaction types:

  • Counterparty - customer or vendor, selected from the Counterparties list.
  • Taxation - sales taxes and VAT.
  • Payments Details. Table attributes:
    • Company contract - agreement between company and company. It is selected from the list of Counterparty Company contracts.
    • Advance - prepayment flag.
    • Document - of the payment. IN case of To Vendor transaction type it is specified only for non-advance payments. IN case of To Customer transaction, it is specified always.
    • Accounts Amount - payment Amount in currency of agreement.
    • Rate - of the currency of agreement. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.
    • Factor - of the rate of agreement currency. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.
    • Payments Amount - payment Amount in currency of the petty cash. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.
    • VAT rate - selected from the VAT rates list.
    • Including VAT - Amount of VAT.
    • Account to be paid - sis specified if the payment is performed for the specific account. Is selected from the documents list "Purchase Invoice For payments" .
    • Order to be paid - displayed in case of payment on specific order.
    • Payments planning document - is selected from the list of the documents  "Banking transfer (plan)" or "Banking receipt (plan)".

For the transaction type "To customer" in case if the peripherals are used the "Receipt" attribute becomes visible. This is the number of the return receipt issued on the fiscal register. Is filled in automatically.

 

 

Attributes required for To advance holder ransaction type:

  • Advance Holder - employee who receives the payment, selected from the Performers list.
  • Document - is filled in case if advance holder has spent more cash assets than indicated in EXPENSE REPORT document. The document is selected from the Expense reports list.
  • Payments planning document - is selected from the list of the documents  "Banking transfer (plan)" or "Banking receipt (plan)".

Attributes required for "Salary of Statusment" transaction type:

 

  • Performer. Performer who gets the cash assets. Is selected from the "Performers" catalog.
  • Business Activity - where payment is attributed according to the cash method of accounting, is selected from the Business Activities list. The attribute is displayed in the form if in the accounting parameters settings the use of cash method of accounting is enabled.
  • Salary. Table attributes:
    • Statement - payroll sheet, according to which cash assets are paid out. It is selected from the Payroll sheets list.
    • Payments Amount - Amount of salary paid in currency of the petty cash.
    • Payments planning document - is selected from the list of the documents  "Banking transfer (plan)" or "Banking receipt (plan)".

Attributes required for "Salary to employee" transaction type:

  • Payments registration period. 
  • Structural unit - for which the payment is attribited. Is selected "Structural units". Is specified only in case of the "Expenses" type of correspondence.
  • Business Activity - where payment is attributed according to the cash method of accounting, is selected from the Business Activities list. The attribute is displayed in the form if in the accounting parameters settings the use of cash method of accounting is enabled.
  • Payments planning document - is selected from the list of the documents  "Banking transfer (plan)" or "Banking receipt (plan)".

     

    Attributes required for Taxes transaction type:

    • Tax Type - selected from the Tax types list.
    • Business Activity - where payment is attributed according to the cash method of accounting, is selected from the Business Activities list. The attribute is displayed in the form if in the accounting parameters settings the use of cash method of accounting is enabled.
    • Payments planning document - is selected from the list of the documents  "Banking transfer (plan)" or "Banking receipt (plan)".

     

    Attributes required for Transfer To Banking Register transaction type:

  • Banking register. Is selected from the "Banking Registers" catalog.
  • Payments planning document - is selected from the list of the documents  "Banking transfer (plan)" or "Banking receipt (plan)".

     

    Attributes required for Other transaction type:

    • Account - where payment is attributed.
    • Business Activity - where payment is attributed, selected from the Business Activities list. It is selected only in case when Account is of Expenses type.
    • Structural unit - where payment is attributed, selected from the Structural unit list. It is selected only in case when Account is of Expenses type.
    • Order - where payment is attributed, selected from the Customer order list. It is selected only in case when Account is of Expenses type.
    • Payments planning document - is selected from the list of the documents  "Banking transfer (plan)" or "Banking receipt (plan)".

    The Petty Banking Expense document can be entered on the basis of the following documents:

    • Supplier invoice.
    • Additional costs.
    • Consignee report.
    • Principal Report.
    • Work order.
    • Invoice for payment (received),
    • Payroll sheet
    • Taxes Accrual
    • Banking Transfer (Plan).
    • Banking Expense (Plan).
