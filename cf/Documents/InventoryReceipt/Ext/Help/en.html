Inventory receipt document

The document is required for registration of received inventory items in warehouse or in production department.

Document can be generated based on the inventory results if required.

Document attributes:

  • Organization - an internal organization issuing the document.
  • Basis - inventory document which has been used for receiving inventory items.
  • Warehouse - structural unit - a warehouse or a production department to receive the inventory.
  • Cell - cell of the warehouse to which the inventory is being received. It is selected from the Cells catalog.
  • Correspondence - GL account of type "Other Income" or "Capital" to define the source of received inventory.
  • Inventory. Tabular section:
    • Products and services - it is selected from the catalog of products and services.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of products and services may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics list. The attribute is displayed in the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of products and services may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches catalog. The attribute is displayed in the form if batches are enabled for products and services in accounting options.

    • Quantity - quantity of products and services.

    • UOM - unit of measure for products and services.

    • Price - price of received products and services.

    • Amount - cost of received products and services.

    • Comment - any additional information.
