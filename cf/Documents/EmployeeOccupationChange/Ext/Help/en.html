Employee occupation change document

The document registers information about changes of position, pay method, or other working conditions of an employee.

 

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Transaction kind - one of the following: Move or modify the terms of payments or Change of Payments Terms.
  • Note - any additional information.

  • Performers - table contains information about new work places and working conditions. Table fields:

    • Performer - employee for whom work place is being changed. It is selected from the Performers list.

    • Employment date - starting this date changes in status of employee will take effect.

    • Structural unit - destination division selected from the Structural units list.

    • Position - new position of the employee. It is selected from the Positions list.

    • Rates - new COUNT of rates taken by the employee. Value is not used for Payroll, but only for analysis of COUNT of rates taken according to the Keep staff schedule.

    • Schedule - new schedule of the employee. It is selected from the Work schedule list.

  • Accruals and Deductions - table contains information about methods of remuneration of employees. The table is always completed when employee is moved to a new position, even if employee pay has not been modified. Otherwise, planned accruals and deductions will be missing for an employee. Table fields:

    • Accrual kind (deduction) - new planned accrual. It is selected from the list of Accrual and deduction types.

    • Amount - new Amount of planned accrual entered in Currency attribute of the table.

    • Currency - currency of planned accrual. It is selected from the Currencies list.

    • GL Expense Account - GL account where costs will be accumulated.

    • A (Actuality) - indicates if record of planned accrual is actual. It required to clear the flag in order to cancel accrual.

  • Belongs to group income taxes - table contains information about planned income taxes. The table is displayed on the form if income tax accounting is enabled in system parameters. Table fields:

    • Accrual kind (deduction) - tax selected from the list of Accrual and deduction types.
    • Currency - income tax currency selected from the Currencies list.

    • А (Actuality) - indicates if record of planned tax is actual. It required to clear the flag in order to cancel taxation.
