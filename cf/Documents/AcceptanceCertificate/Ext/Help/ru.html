Acceptance certificate document

The document Saves information about sales of goods and services to customers.

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.

  • Basis - Invoice for Payments, which was used as a base to generate the document. The attribute is optional.

  • Customer Order - Customer Order document. The attribute is displayed on the form if the accounting option Use customer order for the entire document is enabled. The attribute is optional.

  • Counterparty - customer. It is selected from the list of Counterparties.

  • Company contract - agreement between company and company. It is selected from the list of Company Company contracts.

  • Structural unit - department which is selected from the StructuralUnits/Help">Structural units list from items of the Structural unit type.

To change the price types, discounts and currencies, click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

  • Jobs and toolss. Document table can be filled using pick method (button Pick on the command bar), or data can be entered manually. Table fields:

    • Item - item being sold. It is selected from the ProductsAndServices list with the tools or Job type.

    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of the item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics list. The attribute is displayed on the form if characteristics are enabled for the items in accounting options.

    • Quantity - COUNT of items sold.

    • UOM - unit of measure. It is selected from the UOM Classifier list or from the Units of Measure list.

    • Price - price of item sold.

    • Discount, % - Amount of discount (markup).

    • Amount - cost of item sold.

    • VAT rate - VAT rate selected from the VAT rates list.

    • Including VAT - Amount of VAT.

    • Total - total Amount calculated according to the price and currency settings. To change the settings click on the Edit Prices and Currencies button.

    • Customer Order - Customer Order document. The attribute is displayed on the form if the accounting option Use customer order for every document line. The attribute is optional.

    • Content - text of job / service in the print form. To enable the attribute go to All actions - Change form... and check the flag.

  • Prepayments. The table contains prepayments received from customers earlier. To edit data in Prepayment table, click on the Edit Setoff of advance payment button. Table fields:

    • Document - document with customer prepayment: Petty Banking Receipt, Payment receipt.

    • Order - contains reference to the Customer Order document.

    • Amount of settlements - Amount in contract currency.

    • Rate - rate used to recalculate Amount to document currency.

    • Factor - factor used to recalculate Amount to document currency.

    • Payments Amount - Amount of prepayment in document currency.

  • Additional datarmation. Belongs to group of attributes:

    • Responsible - information about the employee who enters current document. It is selected from the Performers list. The attribute is optional.

    • Note - any additional information.

Acceptance certificate can be entered on the basis of the following documents:

  • Customer Order.

  • Invoice for Payments.
