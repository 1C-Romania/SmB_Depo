Expense report document

Document intends to reflect in accountancy the transactions on the expending by the accountable person of entrusted to him cash or non-cash costs. 

Document is to be designed in the same currency, which is defined for an employee at entrusting funds to him (document "Cash expense" or "Expense") as the currency of the accountable person.

It is necessary to fill in the following requisites:

  • Company. This requisite indicates organization, for which EXPENSE REPORT is done. It has to be chosen from the catalogue "Counterparties". The requisite is displayed in the form if in the settings of accounting parameters  IS chosen several organizations for accounting.
      .

  • Performer. This requisite specifies the accountable person. It is to be chosen in the catalogue "Performers".

  • Note. Specifies comment to the document.

 

At the form of the document there is a button "Edit prices and currency", which opens the dialog for editing of the currency of this document.

Document has several tabular sections, which are located at the corresponding tabs.
Information about the funds received by the accountable person is displayed on the tab "Advances paid." 

 

  • Advances paid. Tabular section:

    • Document for Accountable Banking Out. SELECT either "Cash expense" or "Expense".

    • Amount - expended sum according to the payment document.

 

Data about inventory purchased by the accountable person are to be filled in at the tab "Inventory"

  • Inventory. Tabular section.:

    • Items - items purchased by the accountable person. SELECT from the catalogue "ProductsAndServices"   with the type "Inventory".

    • Characteristic - if necessary this field specifies the item characteristic. Is chosen from the catalogue "Products and services characteristic". Requisite is displayed at the form if in the settings of accountancy parameters the accounting by the item characteristics is on. 

    • Batch - item batch by which item position is taken. Is chosen from the catalogue "Products and services batches". Requisite is not obligatory for filling in.

    • Unit - unit of measurement for item position. Is chosen from the catalogue "UOMClassifier" or "Units of measurement".

    • Quantity- Amount of accepted items which an accountable person is responsible for.

    • Price-price which was paid for the item position buy the accountable person .

    • Amount -the cost of item at this line including taxes accordingly to the ticks put.

    • VAT Rate - value added tax rate. Is to be chosen in the catalogue  "VAT rates".

    • VAT rate Amount - Amount of the value added tax in this line.

    • Total -  field automatically filled in by the sum total of the inventories.

    • Customer order - customer order according to which the inventories have been purchased. SELECT the document "Customer order". Requisite is not obligatory for filling in.

    • Structural unit - warehouse which receives the inventory. SELECT from the catalogue"Structural units"  with the type "Structural unit, warehouse".

    • Cell of the warehouse which receives the inventory. SELECT from the catalogue "Cells". Requisite is not obligatory for filling in .

    • Company contract date of incoming  document - date of incoming document.

    • Number of incoming  document - number of incoming document.

    • Content - presenting the inventory in printing form. To make the requisite visible you must run "All Actions" - "Change form" and put the tick next to the requisite.

    • Banking flow Item for Banking Method Accounting. Is chosen in the catalogue "Banking flow item".  Requisite is displayed at the form if in the settings of accountancy parameters the Banking Method Accounting is on. Requisite is not obligatory for filling in.

 

Data about services purchased by the accountable person are to be filled in at the tab "toolss"

  • toolss. Tabular section:

      • Items - service paid by the accountable person. SELECT from the catalogue "Items"   with the type "tools".

      • Unit - unit of measurement for item position. Is chosen from the catalogue "UOMClassifier" or"Units of measurement". 

      • Quantity- Amount of paid services which an accountable person is responsible for.

      • Price-price of the service.

      • Amount -the cost of the item at this line including taxes accordingly to the ticks put.

      • VAT Rate - value added tax rate. Is to be chosen in the catalogue  "VAT rates".

      • VAT rate Amount - Amount of the value added tax in this line.

      • Total -  field automatically filled in by the sum total of the services.

      • Customer order - customer order according to which the services have been purchased. SELECT the document "Customer order". Requisite is not obligatory for filling in.

      • Structural unit which is expensed. SELECT the catalogue"Structural units"  with the type "Administration and management department" or "tools". Requisite is not obligatory for filling in .

      • Business activity which is expensed. Is chosen from the catalogue "Business activity". Requisite is not obligatory for filling in .

      • Company contract date of incoming  document - date of incoming document.

      • Number of incoming  document - number of incoming document.

      • Content - presenting the service in printing form. To make the requisite visible you must run "All Actions" - "Change form" and put the tick next to the requisite.

      • Banking flow Item for Banking Method Accounting. Is chosen in the catalogue "Banking flow item".  Requisite is displayed at the form if in the settings of accountancy parameters the Banking Method Accounting is on. Requisite is not obligatory for filling in. 

Data about the funds paid by the accountable person to the suppliers are to be filled in at the tab "Payments" .

 

  • Payments details. Tabular section:
    • Counterparty - supplier whom the accountant person have paid to. Is chosen from the catalogue  "Counterparties".
    • Company contract - agreement with a counterparty according to which an accountant person have done the payment. Is chosen from the catalogue "Company contracts".
    • Advance - indication of an advance payment.
    • Document - document according to which payment is done.
    • Amount of settlements  - Amount of the payment. Is specified in the currency of transactions according to the contract with the counterparty.
    • Acquiring Rate - exchangerate of the payments currencyaccording to the contract with the counterparty.Requisite is displayed at the form if in the settings of accountancy parameters the accounting of foreign exchange operations is on. 
    • Multiplicity - multiplicity of the payments currency according to the contract with the counterparty. Requisite is displayed at the form if in the settings of accountancy parameters the accounting of foreign exchange operations is on. 
    • Payments Amount  - Amount of payment. Is specified in the currency of the cash .Requisite is displayed at the form if in the settings of accountancy parameters the accounting of foreign exchange operations is on. 
    • Order - order to the supplier. Is specified in case of  payment of some order. Is chosen the document  "Work order". Requisite is not obligatory for filling in. 
    • Company contract date of incoming  document - date of incoming document.

    • Number of incoming  document - number of incoming document.

    • Banking flow Item for Banking Method Accounting. Is chosen in the catalogue "Banking flow item".  Requisite is displayed at the form if in the settings of accountancy parameters the Banking Method Accounting is on. Requisite is not obligatory for filling in. 
 Expense report may be introduced on the basis of the following documents:
  • "Banking transfer (plan)",
  • "Banking expense (plan)".
