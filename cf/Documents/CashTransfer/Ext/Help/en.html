Cash transfer document 

The document is used for transfers of cash assets.

 

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Currency - selected from the Currencies list.
  • Amount - Amount of transfer in cash assets currency.
  • Item - cash flow item used for transfer. It is selected from the Banking flow item list.
  • Payments Planning Document: is selected from the
    "Banking transfer (plan)" documents list.
  • From. Belongs to group of attributes:
    • Type - method of payment: cash or non-cash.
    • Bank account - bank account of a payer. The field is available when non-cash transfer method is selected. Field value is selected from the Bank accounts list.
    • Petty Banking - petty cash of a payer. The field is available when cash payment method is selected. Field value is selected from the list of Petty Bankinges.
  • To. Belongs to group of attributes:
    • Type - type of cash assets of the recipient: cash or non-cash.
    • Bank account - bank account of a payee. The field is available when non-cash transfer method is selected. Field value is selected from the Bank accounts list.
    • Petty Banking - petty cash of a payee. The field is available when cash payment method is selected. Field value is selected from the list of Petty Bankinges.
  • Note - any additional information.
