Payments order document

This document allows to generate print forms of the bank payment orders.

Document transaction types:

  • Payments.
  • Tax Payments.

The Payments transaction type is used for making a print form of a payment to a company.

The Tax Payments transaction type is used for making a print form of a transfer to budget.

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Transaction kind - of the document.
  • Bank account - for which the print form is generated. It is selected from the Bank accounts list.
  • Recipient - selected from the Counterparties list.
  • Payee account - selected from the Bank accounts list.
  • Payments Type - one of the following: Electronically, By Mail, Telegraph, Urgent.
  • TIN/CIO Payer. Belongs to group of attributes:
    • TIN- payer's identification number.
    • CIO- reason code of registration of a payer.
    • Payer - is displayed in the appropriate field of the print form of the document.
  • TIN/CIO Recipient. Belongs to group of attributes:
    • TIN- payee identification number.
    • CIO- reason code of registration of a recipient.
    • Recipient - is displayed in the appropriate field of the print form of the document.
  • Amount - payment Amount.
  • VAT rate - selected from the VAT rates list.
  • Including VAT - Amount of VAT.
  • Abbreviation of payment purpose - text of the payment destination. It is displayed in the corresponding field of the print form.
  • Note - any additional information.
If the Tax Payments transaction type is selected, the Tax Payments tab becomes available. Tab attributes:
  • Remittal to Budget Type - one of the following: Tax Payments, Customs Payments, Other Payments.
  • Author Group basis - of the company who Creatingd the payment document.
  • KBK - code of the budget classification of revenues of RF.
  • OKATO - payments collector's OKATO code.
  • Payments Basis - conditional notation of the basis of the payment.
  • Fiscal Filter period - conditional notation of the period for which the tax is transferred.
  • Document No. - of transfer of tax or other payment.
  • Company contract date of the Document - of transfer of tax or other payment.
  • Payments Type - conditional notation of the type of tax payment.
The document can be entered on the basis of the following documents:
  • "Work order";
  • "Purchase Invoice For payments";
  • Request for money expense".
