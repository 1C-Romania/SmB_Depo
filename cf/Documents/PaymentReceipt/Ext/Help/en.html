Payment receipt document

The document registers transactions of incoming non-cash assets in the database.

 

Document transaction types:

  • From Customer.
  • From vendor.
  • From advance holder.
  • Currency Purchase.
  • Taxes Return.
  • Other.

The transaction From Customer is used for registration of the customer non-cash payments.

If it is required to receive a payment from vendor for the previously returned items, then the From vendor transaction is selected.

If it is required to accept cash assets from an advance holder - the From advance holder transaction is selected.

In other cases - the Other transaction is selected.

Attributes required for any transaction type:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Transaction kind - of the document. The following items are to be selected from the list: from customer, from vendor, from advance holder, currency purchase, taxes return and other.
  • Bank account - of the Company, for which payment is processed. It is selected from the Bank accounts list.
  • Item - of cash flow, selected from the Banking flow item list.
  • Document Amount - of payment in currency of bank account.
  • Basis - document of the payment.
  • In. Doc. No - number of the incoming documen .
  • In. Doc Company contract date - date of the incoming documen
  • Abbreviation of payment purpose
  • Note - any additional information.

Attributes required for From vendor or From Customer transaction types:

  • Counterparty - customer or vendor, selected from the Counterparties list.
  • Account of Company - selected from the Bank accounts list.

  • Taxation - sales taxes and VAT.

  • Payments Details. Table attributes:
    • Company contract - agreement between company and company. It is selected from the list of Counterparty Company contracts.
    • Advance - prepayment flag.
    • Document - of the payment. IN case of From Customer transaction type it is specified only for non-advance payments. IN case of From vendor transaction, it is specified always.
    • Accounts Amount - payment Amount in currency of agreement.
    • Rate - of the currency of agreement. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.
    • Factor - of the rate of agreement currency. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.
    • Payments Amount - payment Amount in currency of bank account. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options.
    • VAT rate - selected from the VAT rates list.
    • Including VAT - Amount of VAT.
    • Invoice for Payments - selected in case when payment is made towards specific invoice. It is selected from the Invoice For payments list.
    • Order - displayed in case of payment on specific order.
    • Payments planning document - is selected from the list of the documents  "Banking transfer (plan)" or "Banking receipt (plan)".

Attributes required for From advance holder transaction type:

  • Advance Holder - employee from whom the payment is received, selected from the Performers list.
  • Document - of issue of cash assets to an advance folder.
  • Payments planning document - is selected from the list of the documents  "Banking transfer (plan)" or "Banking receipt (plan)".

Attributes required for Currency Purchase transaction type:

  • Account - GL account where payment will be attributed.
  • Acquiring Rate - of the currency. The attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options, and account currency equals to the national currency.
  • Factor - the attribute is displayed on the form if currency transactions are enabled in the accounting options, and account currency equals to the national currency.
  • Amount in the currency of accounting
  • Payments planning document - is selected from the list of the documents  "Banking transfer (plan)" or "Banking receipt (plan)".

Attributes required for Taxes Return transaction type:

  • Accrued tax type - type of the tax which is going to be returned. Is selected from the "Taxes types" list.
  • Business Activity- reversing of expenses by cash method accounting. Is selected from the "Business activities" catalog. The attribute is displayed in the form if in the accounting parameters settings the use of the cash method accounting is enabled.

    Attributes required for Other transaction type:

    • Account - GL account where payment will be attributed.
    • Payments planning document - is selected from the list of the documents  "Banking transfer (plan)" or "Banking receipt (plan)".

    The Payment receipt document can be entered on the basis of the following documents:

    • Services acceptance certificate.
    • Customer invoice.
    • Fixed assets sale.
    • Invoice for Payments.
    • Banking Transfer (Plan).
    • Banking Receipt (Plan).
    • Customer Order.
