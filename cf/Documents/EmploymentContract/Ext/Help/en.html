Employment contract document

The document registers information about hiring of an employee, about his salary and pay methods.

 

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.

  • Note - any additional information.

  • Performers - table contains information about work places and working conditions. Table fields:

    • Performer - employee being hired. It is selected from the Performers list.

    • Employment date - hiring date.

    • Structural unit- destination department selected from the Structural unit list.

    • Position - position of the employee. It is selected from the Positions list.

    • Rates - COUNT of rates taken by the employee. Value is not used for Payroll, but only for analysis of COUNT of rates taken according to the Keep staff schedule.

    • Schedule - schedule of the employee. It is selected from the Work schedule list.

      Accruals and Deductions - table contains information about methods of remuneration of employees. Table attributes:

      • Accrual kind (deduction) - planned accrual. It is selected from the list of Accrual and deduction types.

      • Amount - Amount of planned accrual entered in Currency attribute of the table.

      • Currency - currency of planned accrual. It is selected from the Currencies list.

      • GL Expense Account - GL account where costs will be accumulated.

      • A (Actuality) - indicates if record of planned accrual is actual. It required to clear the flag in order to cancel accrual.

  • Belongs to group income taxes - table contains information about planned income taxes. The table is displayed on the form if income tax accounting is enabled in system parameters. Table fields:

    • Accrual kind (deduction) - tax selected from the list of Accrual and deduction types.
    • Currency - income tax currency selected from the Currencies list.
