Payment receipt (Plan) document

The document is used for planning of cash asset receipts.

 

Document attributes:

  • Company - company, for which the document is Creatingd. It is selected from the Companies list. The attribute is displayed on the form if Accounting by Multiple Companies is enabled in accounting options.
  • Currency - selected from the Currencies list.
  • Amount - planned payment Amount in the currency of planning.
  • Item - cash flow item used for planned cash assets receipt. It is selected from the Banking flow item list.
  • Payments Method - method of payment. One of the following: non-cash, cash or not defined.
  • Counerparty - from which it is planned to receive cash. Is selected from the "Counterparties" list.
  • Company contract -  Is specified the contract with the company according to which the payment is planned to be received. Is selected from the "Company contracts" list.
  • Bank account - bank account of an company. The field is available when non-cash payment method is selected. Field value is selected from the Bank accounts list.
  • Petty Banking - petty cash of an company. The field is available when cash payment method is selected. Field value is selected from the list of Petty Bankinges.
  • Document - basis for planning of cash assets receipt.
  • Note - any additional information.
