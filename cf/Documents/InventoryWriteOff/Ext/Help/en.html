Inventory write-off document 

With the document, you can register inventory write-off from warehouses or production divisions.

The document can be generated based on reconciliation or as required.

Fill out the following document attributes:

  • Organization - an organization issuing the document.
  • Basis - inventory reconciliation document.
  • Warehouse - warehouse, from which the inventory is written off. It is selected from the Structural units list.
  • Cell - cell of the warehouse. It is selected from the warehouse cells list.
  • Correspondence - GL account with type "Other expenses" or "Other current assets".
  • Inventory. Tabular section:
    • Products and services - selected from the catalog of products and services.
    • Characteristic - if necessary, characteristic of products and services may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services characteristics catalog. The attribute is displayed in the form if characteristics are enabled for products and services in accounting options.

    • Batch - if necessary, batch of the products and services item may be selected in this field. It is selected from the Products and services batches catalog. The attribute is displayed in the form if batches are enabled for the products and services items in accounting options.

    • UOM - unit of measure of the products and services item.
  • Comment - any additional information.
