Production document

With this document, you can reflect operations of products assembly in a warehouse or manufacture of products in the production department. 

The document can be generated based on the assembly order, production order and customer order.

Depending on the specified operation kind, the following operations can be registered using the document:

 

  • Assembly -  assembling products in a warehouse or production in the production department. On tab Products, you can specify a structural unit of the company - a warehouse or a production department and also a cell of the structural unit to which manufactured products will be placed. In tabular section Products, specify a list of manufactured products and services. On tab Materials, specify a structural unit of the company - a warehouse or a production department and also a cell of the structural unit from which inventory spent on the products manufacture will be written off. IN tabular section Materials, specify consumed raw materials, materials, semi-finished products spent on the production.

 

  • Disassembly – disassembling raw materials. On tab Products, you can specify a structural unit of the company - a warehouse or a production department and also a cell of the structural unit from which disassembled inventory will be written off. In tabular section Products, you can specify a list of raw materials, which are necessary for disassembly. On tab Materials, you can specify a structural unit of the company - a warehouse or a production department and also a cell of the structural unit to which manufactured products will be placed. IN tabular section Materials, you can specify an output product, i.e. the disassembly result.

Important: Allocation of materials for manufactured products is automatic. You can not edit it manually. Rules of automatic allocation of materials: by specifications and (or) proportionally to the quantity of manufactured products.

 

If you use the mechanism of the inventory reservation, and production is carried out according to customer orders, then the produced goods are reserved for the specified order.

If used materials were previously reserved for the specified customer order, then in the Reserve field, specify an amount consumed from the reserve.

On the Waste tab, you can specify a structural unit of the company - a warehouse or a production department and also a cell of the structural unit to which recyclable waste will be placed. IN tabular section Waste, you can specify recyclable waste that is a result of production and is to be accounted. Waste is entered at zero cost.
