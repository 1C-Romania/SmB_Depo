Inventory reconciliation document 

The document reflects results of a facility/warehouse inventory reconciliation.
It does not initiate any flows, but can serve as a basis for an Inventory receipt document if there is surplus and an Inventory write-off to record shortage.

The document can be filled in automatically, manually or from the product selection form. To complete the document automatically, click Fill out with balance in warehouse. The table area includes goods of the selected structural unit balance. Such goods must correspond with the inventory reconciliation conditions stated in the following fields:

  • Listed products and services. Select one or more products and services.
  • Products and services within group. Select catalog hierarchy groups including products and services.
  • Products and services category. The list of products and services categories classifying specific products and services.

If the condition is not selected, it is not used. 

The document fields include:

  • Organization. The internal organization issuing the document.
  • Warehouse. The structural unit - a warehouse or a department - undergoing the inventory reconciliation.
  • Cell. The warehouse cell undergoing the inventory reconciliation.
  • Inventory. Tabular section:
    • Products and services. Enter products and services catalog item.
    • Characteristic. If necessary, you can specify the products and services item characteristic here. The field is available when the accounting parameters include products-and-services characteristics accounting. Find the value in the Products-and-services characteristics catalog.
    • Batch. The products-and-services item batch. The field is available only when the accounting settings enable products-and-services batch accounting. Find the value in the Batches catalog.
    • UOM. The unit of measure used to enter the products and services quantity. 
    • Deviation. The difference between the actual and accounting data.
    • Quantity. The quantity of UOMs of the products-and-services item according to the inventory reconciliation.
    • Accounting qty. The quantity of UOMs of the products-and-services item according to the accounting.
    • Price. The inventory balance price.
    • Amount. The inventory balance amount according to the reconciliation.
    • Accounting amount. The inventory balance amount according to the accounting.
  • Comment. An arbitrary comment to the document.
