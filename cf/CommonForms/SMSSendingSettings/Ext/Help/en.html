Designed to customize text message sending parameters.

To open the window, in the Administration - Organizer section, click Text message sending settings.

Use text messages to:

  • Inform technical specialists about issues with the 1C databases.
  • Automatically confirm customer payments and other transactions.
  • Inform the customer about events: birthday, next payment date, etc.
  • Send customers bulk notifications of goods, services, discounts and special offers.

To send text messages, make a relevant service agreement with a provider. For more information on the providers' websites, see:

  • Corporate text message services (Beeline Company)
  • MTS communicator (MTS Company).

In the Provide field, enter your provider of the service.

When making an agreement, you get credentials: Login and Password. Specify them in the relevant fields.
