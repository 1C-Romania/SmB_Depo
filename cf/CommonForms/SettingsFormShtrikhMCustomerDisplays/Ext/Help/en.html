Skrikh-M: Customer display driver handler setup form

Form is designed to set the parameters of the customer display, plugged in with driver "Skrikh-M: Customer display driver". The form allows you to configure the following parameters of the device:

  • Model
    Model of connected device.
  • Port
    COM-port, to which device is connected.
  • Timeout
    Time of waiting for response from device.
  • The number of repeats
    The maximum number of attempts to connect to the device
  • Show on the display
    Since the device has only one line, and the majority - two, then you can choose which one to show - the first or the second. The first line usually indicates the product name, and the second - total Amount.

After changing of parameter values, press the button "Record and close" to Save made changes, or the button "Cancel" to refuse their keeping.
