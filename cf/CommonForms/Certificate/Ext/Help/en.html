It is intended to view the main properties of the key's certificate of an digital signature and encryption.

Basic information on the certificate

  • Owner - personal data of the certificate owner;
  • Issued by - information on the certifying center which issued the certificate;
  • Valid until - date of expiry of the certificate;
  • Purpose - types and codes of certificate's use:
    • Signing - the check box is selected if the certificate is used for signature;
    • Encryption - the check box is selected if the certificate is used for encryption.
  • Subject - the list of properties of the certificate subject in familiar names that were identified;
  • Publisher - the list of properties of the certificate publisher in familiar names that were identified;
  • Internal content - structure and values of properties as they are specified in certificate's data.

Exporting the certificate

  • Click Save in file, and then specify the folder to save certificate's data on the computer.

Checking the certificate

  • Click Check to check the validity of the certificate.
