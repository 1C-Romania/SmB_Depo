Pick form

Form is intended for picking inventory in the appropriate tabular section of the document based on their residues, reserves and cost.

To fill tabular section of the document, it is necessary :

  • choose inventory item by double click in the tabular section "Inventory";
  • if the button "Ask COUNT" is pressed in a command bar, then it is required to enter COUNT of selected item, otherwise added COUNT equals 1;
  • selected item will be added into the tabular section "Picked inventory";
  • by pressing button "Ok" data from the tabular section "Picked inventory" will be transferred into the tabular section of the document.
