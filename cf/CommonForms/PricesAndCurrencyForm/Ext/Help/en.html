Setting the order of payment and prices in the document

The additional dialog "Prices and currency" is intended to fill and modify the data about payment order setting and opens by clicking on a button "Edit prices and currency" in the form of the document.

When you open the dialog, values of the parameters set up to the current time, will be displayed. The number and nature of controls in this window depend on the type of document.

This may be such items as:

  • Prices and discounts. Belongs to group of attributes:
    • Kind of the prices - selected from the catalog "Price kinds". Prices of the items, added to the document's specification, will be set based on the selected type of values specified for them ("Retail", "Wholesale" etc.). At any time, you can choose a different type of prices to fill in prices of the document's specification. To modify already filled prices, you need to check the box "Refill prices" in the document.
    • Discount kind - selected from the catalog "Markup and discount kinds". If specification of the document already filled, then recalculation of the document in accordance to a new kind of discount performed only in case, when the flag "Refill prices" is checked.
  • Currency. Belongs to group of attributes:
    • Currency - currency of the document. Selected from the catalog "Currencies". Change of the currency causes automatic recalculation of prices in the document only in case if the flag "Recalculate prices by currency" IsSet.

    • Exchange rate - exchange rate of selected in the document currency. The value of attribute will change only if the currency of document is modified and is available for viewing only.

    • Multiplicity - multiplicity of the selected in the document currency. The value of attribute will change only if the currency of document is modified and is available for viewing only.

  • Accounting currency. Belongs to group of attributes:

    • Currency - accounting currency. Selected from the catalog "Currencies".

    • Exchange rate - exchange rate of selected in the document accounting currency.

    • Multiplicity - multiplicity of the selected in the document accounting currency.

  • Taxes. Belongs to group of attributes:

    • Taxation. Type of tax - sales of goods and services is the value added tax.

    • Amount includes VAT. There are two options filling the Amount VAT in the documents. If this box is checked, then it is assumed, that VAT is already included in the Amount of of the document. IN the column "Total" the Amount of VAT included is shown. VAT is calculated based on the Amount of each item and the VAT rate, specified in the document. If the box is unchecked, then in the Amount of the document (column "Total") VAT is not included.

    • Include VAT in cost. If this box is checked, then at Creation of the posting VAT will be included in the cost.
