Data exchange mechanism. Exchange 1C:Small Business -1C:Accounting.

The data exchange is used to synchronize the changes between 1C:Small Business and 1C:Accounting.

There is a possibility to filter data by companies for data exchange.
The prefix of documents being imported is a letter or groups of letters attached to the beginning of a document number. The prefix is used only for documents whose date is not less than the start date of data exchange. The start date also determines the filtration of the exported documents.Only documents whose date is not less than specified will be exported.
