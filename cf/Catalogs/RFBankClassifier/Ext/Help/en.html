The Russian Bank Classifier stores data of Russian banks. This data is used for filling in bank account details, in documents and other application objects.

You can fill in the Classifier automatically - with the Russian Bank Classifier. Automatic loading of the Bank Classifier greatly simplifies entering information. You can enter bank information into various documents by simply selecting items from the Classifier. It minimizes accounting errors and involves a constantly updated classifier. 

Dissolved banks are inactive in the list.

Use the Banks command of the Catalogs section.

The Classifier indicates:

  • Bank name - a full name of the bank
  • BIC - the Russian Central Bank Identifier Code
  • Corr. account - correspondence account
  • City - a city where the bank is located
  • Address - a full address of the bank

Remember that the bank addresses and phone numbers are loaded only from the 1C:ITS disk.

Use the More - Change form command to display additional information:

  • Bank dissolved - the application register dissolved banks and automatically selects a check box for such banks. You can also select it manually, when you are informed that a bank is dissolved.
  • Bank group - by default, the region where the bank is located.
  • Phone numbers - banks addresses and phone numbers can be loaded only from the 1C:ITS disk.

Grouping banks

  • Banks in the list are grouped by regions. When you open the list, you see only bank regions. Select a necessary region to view its banks. 
  • You can create your own groups in the regions. Click Create group and drag necessary banks there.

Importing bank classifier

The application is delivered with a loaded bank classifier.

  • When the application is started, you can check the relevance of the bank classifier. If it is outdated (not updated for more than 30 days), you will see a message prompting you to update the classifier. Follow the Update bank classifier link.

  • Use the Import classifier command to update the bank data.  

  • Configure automatic classifier update from the RBC website with the scheduled job "Import bank classifier".
  • Sometimes, you do not have to fill in all the fields of a bank card.  You do not fill in the Address and Phone numbers, when the classifier is loaded from the RBC website.
  • When you update data, only edited properties of the classifier are loaded. If the bank BIC is edited, a new entry for this bank will be created. If other fields are edited, they will be overwritten (except for Address and Phone number when the classifier is loaded from the RBC website).

Adding bank

  • If necessary, you can add a bank by clicking Create. Fill in required parameters.
