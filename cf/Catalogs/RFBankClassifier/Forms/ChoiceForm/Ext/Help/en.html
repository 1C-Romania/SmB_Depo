It is intended to select bank data that is used to fill in the details of bank accounts, in documents and other application objects.

The list displays:

  • Bank name - full name of a bank;
  • BIC - BIC of a bank;
  • Corr. account - correspondent account;
  • City - city of location of a bank;
  • Address - full address of a bank.

Discontinued banks are hidden by default. To see the list of these banks, run the More - Show discontinued banks command.

To update bank data, run the Export classifier command.

Bank selection

  • Select the bank you need.
  • Click Select.
  • You can also double-click.
