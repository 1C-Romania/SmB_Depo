Bank properties description:

  • Bank activity discontinued - application takes into account the discontinued banks and selects the check box for such banks automatically. You can also select a bank manually if you have information about its closing.
  • Bank name - full name of a bank.
  • Corr. account - correspondent account.
    BIC - BIC of a bank.
  • City - city of location of a bank.
  • Group of banks - default region where bank is located. You can include bank into a new group. Click Show all, select the group you need from the list. Click Create to enter new group.
  • Address - full address of a bank.
  • Phone numbers - you can export addresses and phone numbers of banks from 1C:ITS disk only or enter them manually.
