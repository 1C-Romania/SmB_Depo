Counterparty price kinds catalog

The vendors and competitors price types are Saved in this list.

 

Counterparty's price lists are generated by the counterparty price types. Every column defines prices of the specific type. Different price types may be compared. Thus each ProductsAndServices position can have so many counterparty prices, as many counterparty price kinds are registered in the Infobase.

 

Prices are used as default prices in Supplier invoices and Job orders.
