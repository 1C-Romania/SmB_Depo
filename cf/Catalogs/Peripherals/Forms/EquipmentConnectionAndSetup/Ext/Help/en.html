Form of the setting and connection of the peripherals

This form is designed to connect and configure the external equipment connected in the catalog "External Equipment".
To connect and configure the equipment it is necessary to perform the following steps:

  • Install the necessary drivers
  • On the corresponding tabs in the table fields it is necessary to specify the lines matching the necessary equipment;
  • Activate a line containing information about the external hardware and click "Customize";
  • Set the equipment patameters.
