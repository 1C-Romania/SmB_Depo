How to specify the participants in the agreement for the direct exchange between counterparties in the correct way?

In the agreement of the direct exchange with counterparties fix the following information:

  • choose "Company";
  • specify "Company" и "Partner";
  • specify "Company identifier" and "Companty identifier". By default identifiers are automatically generated by the application on the basis of TIN and CRR of the organization and the counterparty.

Important. For the exchange of electronic documents the TIN and CRR of the organization and counterparties must be correctly filled in in the corresponding elements of the catalogues.

To fill in the the data on the organization in the agreement quickly it is recommended to use "Typical agreements on the use of electronic documents". The application will automatically fill in the data on the organization and on the types of electronic documents if you choose a typical agreement.

Which methods of direct exchange of electronic documents with counterparties does the application support?

Methods for the direct exchange which can be specified in the agreement (tab "Exchange Method"):

  • "Through the catalogue" - in this case it is necessary to the specify the directory for outgoing electronic documents sent to the counterparty and the directory for incoming electronic documents received by the organization .
  • "Via email " - in this case an email address of the counterparty to which electronic documents will be sent specifies in the requisite "Counterparty Email Address" and requisite "Email Account of the counterparty" specifies an email account of organization which will be used for sending electronic documents and for receiving the incoming data ;
  • "Through FTP" - in this case specify the FTP-server parameters to authorize on it, then specify the directory for outgoing electronic documents sent to the counterparty and the directory for incoming electronic documents received by the organization.
Important. If the application uses email for exchange, it is recommended to use a separate email account, Creatingd specially for exchange.

How to enable an encryption of the transmitted data at the direct exchange with the counterparties?

To start using the electronic documents encryption in a direct exchange with counterparties it is necessary to:

  • Enable the use electronic digital print full name (EDS) in the general system settings;
  • Perform general cryptography setup in the system;
  • Specify in the field "Certificate details" - an item from the list "Certificates of EDS' '(certificate of organization) in the form of agreement;
  • In the form of agreement specify in the field "Encryption Certificate" - the certificate file of the counterparty (the public key received from the counterparty through the open communication channels).

    The certificate public key is necessary to send to the counterparty so that he could encrypt the transmitted for the organization.
    Encryption and details certificates are valid for all types of electronic documents involved in the direct exchange.

    Important. For cryptographic operations it is enough just of the public part of the print full name key (print full name certificate), to decrypt - always use a private part of the key which is recommended to keep in a safe place for the security reasons.

    Which documents of the application can participate in a direct exchange of electronic documents with counterparties?

    In the agreement on the tabs "Outgoing documents" and "Incoming Documents" it is necessary to specify which types of electronic documents will be transmitted between the parties.

    How to configure the use of EDS and receipts on the delivery confirmation for a specific type of electronic document?

    In the agreement on the appropriate tabs "Outgoing documents" and "Incoming Documents" configure the use of EDS and control of the delivery package up to the kind of electronic document.
    Depending on the settings the list of the planned statuses (processing steps) will be changing for each type of electronic document.
    Important. To use a digital print full name in the general settings of the system enable the possibility of using electronic digital print full name (EDS) and perform general cryptography setup in the system.
