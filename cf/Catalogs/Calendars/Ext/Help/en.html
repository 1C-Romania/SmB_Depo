You can use the list enables to keep work schedules.

The application allows forming and saving work schedules used by a company. The work schedules are generated according to the Business calendars. There is an option to use work schedules for the whole company and its departments (for example, warehouse work schedule). The department's work schedule contains the information on working days of this department. For example if a warehouse receives products from suppliers on Tuesdays and Thursdays, then the working days for Products receipt at warehouse work schedule will be Tuesdays and Thursdays.

Depending on the production need or depending on the demands of departments, for which work schedule is intended, the work schedules can be set up either for the whole year or for a certain period of time. You can aso set up work schedule from time to time during the whole year several days in advance.

The list displays:

  • Description - the user fills this field when entering the schedule;
  • Business calendar - the user specifies the basis for entering the work schedule.
  • Filling method - the kind of schedule. Depending on the filling method the schedules may be of two kinds:
    • Weekly (hourly) - such schedule is strictly linked to week days. It is the simplest and most popular work schedule. The 40-hour working week schedules and others are the calendars of this kind. Such schedules differ only in the number of working hours or days. This work schedule has several variables: five-day working week, six-day working week, two-days working week.
    • By random length cycles. Such schedule is strictly linked to dates, as a certain date from which the days are counted is specified as a cycle start. Week days do not influence the schedule, holidays are taken into account very seldom. Cyclic schedules have some variations, such as shift schedules and schedules with floating weekends, e.g. one working day after three days off, two working days after two days off and so on.
  • Possible reason - the result of schedule adequacy check. Error warning is displayed during work schedules filling.
  • Description - detailed description is filled when entering.

Wrongly filled schedules are marked with red color.

Configuring work schedules

  • Enter the number of work schedules you need according to the business calendar.
  • For each work schedule, specify the working (active) days. Fill work schedule several days ahead according to your needs.
  • In the end of the year it is necessary to fill in the available work schedule several days ahead based on the past year data for the next year, in order not to make mistakes when calculating certain documents (for the period before the publication of the next year federal calendar).

Entering new work schedule

Click Create, and then fill in the required fields.

Grouping work schedules

If there is a lot of work schedules in the list, you can combine them into groups. The work schedules can be grouped according to different attributes, e.g. according to the filling method.

  • To enter a group click , enter a group description. There is an option to subordinate one group to another. To do this, select the parent group when entering.
  • Enter the number of groups you need, drag work schedules to relevant groups using a mouse.
