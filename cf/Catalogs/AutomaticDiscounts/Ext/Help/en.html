Automatic discounts and markups catalog

The Automatic Discounts and Extra Charges Kinds catalog is intended to describe all possible discuounts used by the company and to define rules of their interaction. The final elements of the catalog are discounts and extra charges. Every group of the catalog is a category and it defines rules of common use of discounts inside this category. The following rules may be defined as the rules of common use:

  • Addition - the sum of all discounts inside the group is applied
  • Min - the smallest discount inside the group is applied
  • Max - the biggest discount inside the group is applied
  • Sequential application - all discounts are applied one by one in the order they are difined in the category
  • Replacement - when several discounts (extra charges) are used jointly in one group, only such discount will be applicable which has the highest priority in the group

You may choose the following settings for every discount (exctra charge):

· type of presentation: persentage or amount

· place of presentaiton: in a document and in a string

· sphere of application of the discount: in wholesale trading, retail trading or both

· discount - percentage or amount of the discount

· currency in which the discount amount is presented

· discount conditions upon which the discount is applied. You may set "one-time sale volume" or "amount of purchase" as the condition

·  refinement of the discount amount up to products and services (group with account of the hierarchy), a products and services category or price category

· restriction of discount recipients: the list of counterparties for wholesale trading and the list of structural items (stocks) for retail trading. If no restriction is set, the discount is valid for all recipients

· discount application timetable in terms of time and weekdays. If no timetable is set, the discount is always valid

You may work with the catalog in the Discounts and extra charges kinds workplace which is called from the Sales section.

You may also edit types of (not automatic) discounts and extra charges in this workplace.

Important. The possibility to work with automatic discounts is defined by accounting settings. Go to the Settings section, then to Sales. The Use automatic discounts and extra charges checkbox defines the possibility to use automatic discounts.
