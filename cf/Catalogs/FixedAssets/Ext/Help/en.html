Fixed assets catalog

The information about fixed and intangible assets of the Companies is Saved in this list.

The information editing is done in a separate window, which is opened from the list form.

The following information is entered:

  • Type. The type is selected from the list: fixed asset or intangible asset.
  • Depreciation method. The method is selected from the list: linear or proportionally to finished goods volume.
  • Unit of Measure. The field is filled if depreciation method proportionally to finished goods volume is selected (selected from the UOM Classifier).
  • Initial cost. The initial cost in the accounting currency.
  • GL account. The account is selected from the Managerial chart of accounts (required field).
  • Depreciation account. The account is selected from the Managerial chart of accounts (required field).
