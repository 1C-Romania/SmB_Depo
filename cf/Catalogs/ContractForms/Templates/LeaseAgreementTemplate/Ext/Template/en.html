2. RIGHTS AND OBLIGATIONS OF THE PARTIES

 

2.1. The Lessor agrees to:

2.1.1. Transfer the Leased Property specified in paragraph 1.4 of this Agreement to the Lessee in due time. Specified obligation shall be deemed fulfilled after the Leased Property is transferred to the Lessee for tenure and use and the Transfer and Acceptance Act is signed by the Parties.

2.1.2. To effect major repairs of the Leased Property at his own expense if such repair is of urgent necessity.

2.2. The Lessor has the right to:

2.2.1. Request for early termination of the Agreement in case the Lessee uses the Leased Property other than as intended.

2.2.2. Request for early termination of the Agreement in case the Leased Property is in a Status unsuitable for use.

2.3. Lessee agrees:

2.3.1. To pay leasing fees in due time.

2.3.2. Not to carry out reconstruction, re-equipment and other capital works without the written permission of the Lessor.

2.3.3. To ensure Lessor representatives the unimpeded access to the leased units in order to perform scheduled (no more than once every two months) examinations and s.

2.3.4. Maintain the Leased Property in a working condition, to effect the repair at his own expense and incur expenses for the property maintaining.

2.3.5. Return to the Lessor the Leased Property in the same condition as it was received taking into account standard wear and tear, at the Agreement termination. Specified obligation shall be deemed fulfilled after the Leased Property is returned to the Lessor and the Transfer and Acceptance Act is signed by the Parties.

2.4. The Lessee has the right:

2.4.1. When finding deficiencies in the Leased Property, fully or partially impeding the use thereof, at his own option:

- To require the Lessor either to eliminate all defects of the property at Lessor's own expence or to reduce leasing fee reasonably or compensate Lessee's expenses incured for the property defects elimination;

- To withhold an Amount of expenses incurred to eliminate these defects from the leasing fee giving prior notice to the Lessor;

- To request for early termination of the Agreement.

2.4.2. To underlet (sublease) the Leased Property with prior written consent of the Lessor and assign his rights and obligations under this Agreement to another person (transfer of lease), provide the Leased Property for free use as well as to pledge lease rights and contribute these rights to the charter capital of business partnership and companies or as a share to a production cooperative.

2.4.3. Upon the expiration of this Agreement the Lessee has priority right under otherwise equal conditions over other persons to conclude an agreement for a new term. 2.5. The Parties shall inform each other in case their legal address fax number, phone, bank account data are changed, no later than within 74 hours from the date of the change.
