Price kinds catalog

The selling price types are Saved in this list.

 

Price list is generated on the basis of the selling price types. Every column defines price of the specific type. Prices of different types can be compared. Thus each ProductsAndServices position have so many prices, as many price kinds are registered in the infobase.

 

Selling prices are used as default prices in the shipping documents, in Customer order. Some of the selling price types has a special purpose in the system. This is the price type, which contains data not about the prices for customers, but it contains a value of the Item Accounting price. The accounting price can be used, for example, as a ProductsAndServices Planned Cost Price.

 

The price can be specified manually or it can be calculated. The dynamic prices are calculated in the sales documents on the basis of the base prices, incremented by percent. A method of recalculation from the base price can be Saved for the calculated price type. Prices may be periodically recalculated in the data processor Pricing.

 

The price display format in the Price list can be defined for a price.
