Discount cards catalog

The catalog contains information about customers' discount cards.

You can read the information from a card using a magnetic stripe reader or barcode reader.

Most of the discount card settings depend on the kind of a discount card.

The kind of a discount card is specified as an item of the Discount card kinds catalog when entering customer's discount card information.

Depending on the chosen kind of a discount card, you can edit some attributes.

If you select the Membership card check box for a discount card, you need to fill out the Card holder field. Select the counterparty to which the card was issued as a discount card holder.

If you select Barcode kind of the discount card, you need to fill out the Barcode field.

If you select Magnetic kind of the discount card, you need to fill out the Magnetic code field.

If you select Mixed kind of the discount card, you need to fill out the Barcode and Magnetic code fields.

In case a magnetic code and barcode are the same, use a key that copies barcode into magnetic code and vice versa. The keys are located between these fields.

The Description field is filled out automatically, and you can change it manually.

You can set the option to work with customers' discount cards in the accounting settings.

Go to the Settings section, and then Sales. To enable the option to use the discount cards, select the Use discount cards check box.

Discount cards are presented by the customer upon payment. The customer is allowed a discount according to the submitted discount card. Amount of the discount, which is automatically allowed to the customer upon the presentation of the discount card, is defined by the kind of the discount card. Certain percentage of the discount can be applied to a certain discount card or depending on the kind of the discount card, e.g. "Silver", "Gold", "Platinum".

Depending on the kind of a discount card, fixed or progressive discount can be provided.
