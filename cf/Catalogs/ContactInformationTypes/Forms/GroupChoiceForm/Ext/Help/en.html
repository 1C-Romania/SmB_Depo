The list is used to select the kind of contact information.

You can open it by clicking the Select button on the input window for a new field of contact information.

Kinds of contact information are predetermined groups, at that, each group corresponds to the list or document being the container of contact information, and items of these groups specify the content of contact information for the application objects. At that, you can not change the content of the groups. The list of the groups has hierarchical structure in the form of a tree.

Selecting the contact information kind

  • Select the group you need, and then click Select.
