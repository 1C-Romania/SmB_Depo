Printing the contact information of counterparties

The form is intended to generate contact information of counterparties and display this information, print, print to a file, or send by email.

You can open the form by running the Print contact information command on the counterparty's card or from the list of counterparties.

In the settings area, located on the right, you can set the printing mode: cards or list. IN the below area the list of companies is specified for printing. You can select a single company or the groups of companies. IN the latter case, all companies included in the group will be printed hierarchically.

Contact information kinds to be printed can be specified by clicking the Setting printing structure hyperlink. The settings are saved for each user individually.

Description of the settings panel commands:

  • Print. Printing the generated print form.
  • Save. Saving the print form in the selected formats into a folder on a hard drive.
  • Send. Sending the print form as an attachement to email to specified addresses. You need to configure the email account first.
  • Update. Creating the print form for the selected counterparties.
