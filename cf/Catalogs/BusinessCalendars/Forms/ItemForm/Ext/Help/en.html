Viewing or creating the production calendar.

Business calendar is a sort of classifier, constant data that does not change during the year.  

The Russian national production calendar is included in the delivered application data and updated according to amendments in the Russian national holidays.

If necessary, you can maintain multiple production calendars. Different regions may have different holidays and, therefore, different extra working days.

It is recommended to introduce production calendar according to the national production calendar for the entire year. That will prevent calculation errors during the year. Business calendar itself is not used for accounting. On its basis, you can create work schedules of your departments and work schedules for your employees. 

Entering production calendar

  • Fill in the production calendar title:
    • Name
    • Code- no more than three characters
    • Year - by default, the current year
  • Click Change day, arrange the calendar days.
    • Day kinds of the production calendar are highlighted with colors:
      • black – a working day
      • dark red – Saturday
      • red – Sunday
      • dark blue– a day before a public holiday
      • pink – a public holiday
    • By default, a new production calendar is filled in with non-working days.

Transferring public holiday

You can use this option if the calendar data is necessary, but it is not yet officially adopted and the application does not have it yet, i.e. some transfers may not be approved by the government. You may fill in the transfers in advance, and later, when the national calendar data is approved and added to the application, the transferred dates will be corrected automatically.

  • In the production calendar, select the holiday that you want to transfer.
  • Click Transfer day.
  • Select the new date in the calendar and click OK.
  • The data about transferred days is displayed in the right part of the production calendar, in the Transferred days list.

Filling out by default

  • Use the Fill out by default command to add a calendar filled in according to the national production calendar from the application delivery package. 
  • For the current year, the production calendar is generated on the basis of confirmed data about working days, holidays and transferred public holidays. For future years - according to the Russian Labor Code (for the Russian production calendar).

Printing production calendar

  • Click Print to display the production calendar summary for a specified year.
    • You can display a working time balance for a year, quarter, or six months.
    • You can see statistic data for the quantity of calendar days, working days, and holidays and working time in hours - for 40-, 36-, or 24-hour working week.
