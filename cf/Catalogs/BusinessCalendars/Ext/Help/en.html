It is intended to form and store the production calendars.

You can open the catalog in the Catalogs section by running the corresponding command.

The production calendar is a kind of classifier, constant data of which is not changed whithin a year. There can be several calendars as holidays and holidays shifts are different in various regions.

The business calendar of the Russian Federation (which is nation-wide) is included into the delivery package of the application and is updated upon the approval of the calendar and holiday shifts by the government. You can enter several production calendars if necessary. It is recommended to enter a production calendar at the beginning of the year on the basis of a national calendar for the whole year at once. It will help to avoid errors in calculations during the year.

Business calendars are not used in the accounting. The departments work schedules, as well as employees work schedules are formed basing on the business calendar.
