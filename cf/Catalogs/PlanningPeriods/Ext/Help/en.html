Planning periods catalog

The planning periods are Saved in this list. For example, it Saves planning periods of sales, and also options of the different planning scenarios, for example: optimistic, realistic and pessimistic.

 

There is the special predefined item Actual for registration in the system of actual data. This item can not be modified.

A catalog item has the following attributes:

  • Beg Company contract date. The planning period start date.
  • End date. The planning period end date.
  • Filter periodicity. The field is used to specify planning periodicity: day, week, ten days, month, quarter, half a year and one year. Thus, it is possible, to represent planning period for a year with departments by quarters, for example.
