Fixed assets report

The report is available in two options:

  • Depreciation accrued
  • Inventory card

Depreciation accrued


For the selected period of time you can use the report to get the information on fixed assets cost as of the beginning and end of the period, as well as their change and accumulated depreciation as of the beginning and end of the period, receipts (increments) and expenses (decrement) of the depreciation amounts.

Inventory card
 

The report contains consolidated data on the cost and depreciation of fixed assets.

You can use the report to get the information on the initial cost of fixed assets, amount of the accrued depreciation and depreciated cost of fixed assets for the date specified.
