Payment orders report

The report allows to analyze how customers’ orders are executed in what concerns payment.

The following indicators are shown in the report:

  • Order Amount – the Amount which is specified in the customer order.
  • Prepayment - the Amount of advance payment by order.
  • Payments - the payment Amount by order.
  • Total - total Amount of advance payment and payment by order.
  • Left to pay / overpaid – the Amount which must be paid, to pay for the order completely or the overpayment Amount.
