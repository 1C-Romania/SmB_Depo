Payment of bills report

The report allows to analyze payment of customers invoices.

 

In the report the following indicators are shown:

  • Invoice Amount – the Amount specified in the invoice for payment.
  • Prepayment - the Amount of advance by order.
  • Payments - the payment Amount by order.
  • Total - total Amount of advance payment and payment by order.
  • Left to pay / overpaid – the Amount which must be paid, to pay the invoice completely or the overpayment Amount.
