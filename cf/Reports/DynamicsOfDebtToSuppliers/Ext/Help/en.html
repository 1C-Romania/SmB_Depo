Dynamics of debts to suppliers report

The report provides the analysis of dynamics of total and overdue debts to suppliers for the specified period with respect to the selected interval.

The report rows can be detailed by the Debts to suppliers by debt due dates report, getting the detailed information on counterparties and debts due dates.

The report interval is specified in the settings. The interval is defined automatically by default, depending on the selected report period. For example, when forming a monthly report, the interval is set to Day, and when forming an year report – Month.

The data is included in the report in the currency of management accounting.
