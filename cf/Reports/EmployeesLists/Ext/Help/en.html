Employees report

The report has the following options:

  • Employees list
  • Planned accruals to employees
  • Passport data of employees
  • Contact information of employees

Employees list


The report contains data on employees working at the company.

Planned accruals to employees


The report contains data on the planned accruals to employees.

Passport data of employees


The report contains data on passports of employees.

Contact information of employees


The report contains data on contact details of individuals connected with employees.
