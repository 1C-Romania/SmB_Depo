Advance holders payments report

The report shows within the selected period of time the state and changes of advance holders debts resulted from the settlements with advance holders. The debt is changed by the Expense report and Cash payment documents. You can open the report by clicking the corresponding hyperlink on the actions panel.
 
You can group the debts by employees and currencies in the report. The report may be generated in two modes: Statement and Balances. IN the Balances mode the report will not contain data on the receipts and expenses of cash, it will contain only the debts for the selected date.

Each mode of the report may be generated in currency or in the currency of accounting.
