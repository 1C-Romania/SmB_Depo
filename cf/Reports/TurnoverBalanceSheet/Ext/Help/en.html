Turnover balance sheet report

The report contains information about opening and closing balances, debit and credit turnovers of all GL Accounts in specified period.

 

The Statusment may be generated by selected Company.

To Creating the report click "Generate" button.

Report attributes:

  • Filter period - start and end dates.
  • Company - Company, whose data is used to generate the report.
  • GL Account - GL Account, used for generating the report. If it is empty - all accounts are displayed.
  • Currency amount - if the flag is marked - currency Amounts will be displayed for currency accounts.
