Incomplete production report

The report shows information about the enterprise direct and indirect production costs. The data is grouped by departments with details by customer orders.

The report can be shown in two variants:

  • Unallocated costs
  • Balances of unallocated costs

    Unallocated costs


    The report displays information about balances and movements of production costs.

    Unallocated costs balance


    The report shows information on production costs balance.
