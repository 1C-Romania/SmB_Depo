Plan/actual analysis of output cost report

You can use the report to analyze the ratio of planned and actual quantity and cost of output, work performed, services rendered.

The report contains the indicators as follows:

  • Quantity of products – quantity of goods produced, work performed, services rendered.
  • Actual – quantity and production cost of goods.
  • Plan – quantity and production cost calculated according to the production specification. The cost is calculated as a multiplication of unit actual production cost and quantity of planned expenses.
  • Deviation - difference between the quantity and cost of actual and planned expenses.
