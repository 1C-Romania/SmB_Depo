Cash assets report

The report displays cash flow – both cash and non-cash. Use the relevant link on the action panel to generate the report.
 
The report may be generated in two modes: Statement and Balances. Cash flow items are displayed for the selected period with details on petty cashes and bank accounts in the Statement mode. IN the Balances mode, data about receipts and expenses is excluded. Only cash assets balance amounts in bank accounts and in petty cashes of the company are displayed.

Each mode of the report may be generated in currency or in the currency of accounting.
