Purchase order analysis report

The report allows analyzing the content and the progress of purchase order regarding shipment and payment.


Regarding shipment by order such indicators are shown:

    • Ordered – COUNT of goods which is specified in the purchase order.
    • IsReceived - the COUNT of goods received by order.
    • Left to receive - COUNT of goods left to receive by order.
    • Come to reserve by order - COUNT of goods which is reserved under the customer order.
    • Enter for warehouse - COUNT of goods which enters as the free balance

Regarding payment of goods by order the following indicators are shown:

  • Document Amount – the Amount specified in the Purchase order.
  • Planned - the Amount planned by order on the Payments calendar tab in the order and by documents of cash flow planning.
  • Prepayment - the advance Amount by order.
  • Payments - the payment Amount by order.
  • Total - total Amount of the advance and payment by order.
  • Left to pay – the Amount which must be paid to pay for the order completely.
