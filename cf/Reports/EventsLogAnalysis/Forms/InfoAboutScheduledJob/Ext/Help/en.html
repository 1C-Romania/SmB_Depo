It enables you to view statistical data on the selected scheduled job.

You can open it by double-clicking the scheduled job name in the Event log analysis report - Duration of the scheduled jobs.

The report displays the following:

  • Total duration
  • Number of launches
  • Number of cancelled jobs
  • Number of errors

It also provides information about scheduled job sessions, their duration and time.

Changing schedule of scheduled jobs

  • Click Change schedule, fill out the necessary parameters.

Viewing event log

  • Click Go to registration log to view data about scheduled job sessions in the registration log.
