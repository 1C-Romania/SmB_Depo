Discounts applied in document report

The report is intended to analyze the automatic discounts (margins) applied to a certain document.
You can open the report by selecting the Use automatic discounts and margins functional option (Settings - Sales section).

Report features:

  • Grouping all automatically applied discounts (margins) according to the rules of their use;
  • Analysis of all not applied discounts (margins) due to the breach of the rules of their use;
