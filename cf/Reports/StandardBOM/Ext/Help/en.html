Standard bill of materials report

The report provides user-friendly presentation of content and production technology of the finished goods based on specifications.

The report contains a list of components with quantities required for manufacturing of the selected product. The components that have to be manufactured according to their own specifications are highlighted with another color in report.
At the bottom of the report the list of technological operations, required for manufacturing of such components and the product itself, is displayed. Next to each operation the time norm in hours is displayed.
