Timesheet report

The report displays summarized data of the working hours accounting based on documents Time sheet. The report can be generated in two modes: By Days and Totally.

In the By days mode, the report displays data on working time daily expenses for the specified period. The data is grouped by departments.


In the Totally mode, the report shows data on summary expenses of working time detailed to a type of working hours for the specified period. Data is grouped by employees and departments.
