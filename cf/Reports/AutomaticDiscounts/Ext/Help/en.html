Automatic discounts report

The report is intended for the analysis of automatic discounts applied in documents.

You can open the report by clicking the hyperlink in the Analysis section.

Information in the report is presented in a tabular form. The information on the discount recipient, discount and products and services document is displayed in rows, while the automatic discount kind is displayed in columns. The amount of a discount is displayed on the crossing of rows and columns.

Information is included into the report after the registration of sales with the Customer invoice, Acceptance certificate, Work order, Processing report or CR receipt.

The CR receipt for return with the Return from the customer operation kind reduces the amount of automatic discounts.
