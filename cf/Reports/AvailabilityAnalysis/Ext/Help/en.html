Inventory availability analysis report

You can use the report to analyze the inventory balances, reserves and available goods quantity.

The report has vertical and horizontal groupings. The structural units of the company are grouped vertically. Horizontally the report is grouped by companies and products and services items. Thus, you can use the report to determine the number of the reserved goods by all structural units in total, and estimate the reserves and available balance of the company.

The fact of reservation and placement is registered by the documents generated with the specification of the Customer order document. There is an option to configure the report showing the reserved goods for each structural unit.
