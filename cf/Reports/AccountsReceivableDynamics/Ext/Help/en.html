Accounts receivable dynamics report

The report provides the dynamics analysis of total and overdue customer debts for the specified period with respect to the selected interval.

The report rows can be detailed by the Customer debts by debt due dates report, getting the detailed information on counterparties and debt due dates.

The report interval is specified in settings. The interval is defined automatically by default depending on the selected report period. For example, when forming a monthly report the interval is set to Day, and when forming a year report – Month.

The data is included into the report in the currency of management accounting.
