Purchasing subsystem

To support goods purchasing process, services and operations IS following opportunities:

  • Vendors registration and contact information,
  • Vendors orders appearance and control of their execution,
  • The Creation of supplies schedule.

The inventory receipt of on the enterprise can be reflected according to different schemes:

  • receipt for company's payment,
  • acquisition by the advance holder,
  • reception for realization from the commissionaire,
  • receiving of commission materials and materials for processing.

The function of receipt registration of Additional costs on inventory acquisition is supported.
