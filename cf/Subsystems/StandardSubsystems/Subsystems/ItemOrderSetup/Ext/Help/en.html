For the series of objects the configuration allows to configure the order of elements in the lists. For placement of elements are used the buttons Up and Down of the list forms, which are available in the command panel of the form. For hierarchical and subordinate lists the elements are moved within its group.

In the series of cases the Up and Down buttons do not work. The placement of the elements is not possible:

  • If the list is under selection and thus IS not all the elements are displayed in the list;
  • if the list is arranged not by the order of elements but otherwise - for example, by the name of the list items.

In such cases when the Up and Down commands are executed the warning appears.
