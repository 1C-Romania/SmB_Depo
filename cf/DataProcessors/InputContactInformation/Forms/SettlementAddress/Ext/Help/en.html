The form is used to enter settlement according to a detailed template.

It is opened using the Select button in the City, Settlement field of the address entry template.

In the Area field it is necessary to enter the constituent company of the Russian Federation where the settlement or city is located. If the address classifier for the constituent company of the Russian Federation is not filled in and the current user have rights to export the classifier, it will be suggested to export the classifier when filling in the Area field. If the classifier is exported, there is also an option to choose the part of the address from the list by clicking Select.

The District, Reqion, City, City Area, Settlement fields are used to enter the required parts of the address. Fields to be filled in depends on the address. For example, in some addresses district is not included, the others are entered with city or settlement field empty. If old (historic) name of the settlement is selected, the application displays the message. To confirm the selection, click Yes, and then continue data entry. To avoid displaying of the outdated addresses in the lists, it is recommended to select the Hide outdated addresses check box when setting this type of contact data. After the city or settlement is entered, click OK, and then continue to enter the address.

If the address classifier is exported for the region, then when entering data in each field the variants for the corresponding parts of the address will be suggested taking into account previously filled fields.
