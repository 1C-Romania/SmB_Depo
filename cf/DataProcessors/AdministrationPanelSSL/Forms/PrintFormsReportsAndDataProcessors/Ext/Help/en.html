Use this panel to configure print forms, report variants, report sending, additional reports and data processors. 

  • Print form templates
    		• List of templates for configuring the print forms generated from the application.
  • Report variants
    		•  List of report variants for customizing their accessibility and placement in the application sections.
  • Additional reports and data processors
    		•  Connection of external print forms, reports and data processors in addition to the ones provided in the application.
