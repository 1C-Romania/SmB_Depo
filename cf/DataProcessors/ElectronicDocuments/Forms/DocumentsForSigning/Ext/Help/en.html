Viewing all electronic documents pending signature

The responsible employee can work with unsigned electronic documents in a separate form opened by clicking Documents for signature.

Form content and actions with a form
  • The form contains a list of all digital signature certificates installed on the computer and used to sign electronic documents; it also specifies a number of electronic documents to sign with each signature certificate.
  • Use the form to sign all electronic documents with the certificate (Sign all with certificate). The secret key to the private part of the digital signature certificate is specified just once.
  • Click Go to document list to open the electronic document list and view each document as a print form of the infobase document. Then, mark the necessary documents and sign them (click Sign marked).
  • Click Return to certificate list to return to the list of available digital signature certificates.

IMPORTANT. If you open the form when only one digital signature certificate is available, you immediately open the list of electronic documents pending signature.
