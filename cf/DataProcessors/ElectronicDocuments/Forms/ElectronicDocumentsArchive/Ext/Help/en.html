Content and functions of electronic document archive form

  • The form contains an electronic document archive with the Incoming and Outgoing tabs, and also lists of electronic documents packages.
  • In the electronic document archive, you can set filter by a responsible employee, kind and status of electronic documents.
  • In the list, you can run the standard list commands and view the electronic document form.
  • Click Send selected packages on the Unsent packages tab to send a specific package out of turn.
  • Click Unpack selected packageson the Unpacked packages tab to unpack a specific package (you may need it, when you encrypt packages for direct exchange).
  • The All Packages tab contains all generated or received packages of electronic documents. Data files of already processed packages (delivered to the receiver or successfully unpacked) can be deleted by clicking All Actions - Delete files of processed packages.

Viewing content of electronic document package

You can view content of an electronic document package after you save the package file on the drive.
To export a group of electronic document package files, select the necessary packages from the list on the All Packages tab and click All actions - Save ED packages on drive. Similar command Save package on drive is available in the form of the Electronic document package document.

Storage of created electronic documents

Once generated, all electronic documents get to archive of outgoing electronic documents.
To open the archive form, click Electronic document archive.

Decryption of received data
  • If the direct exchange agreement between counterparties involves document encryption, received data as encrypted packages will be placed to the Electronic document archivelist on the Packed packages tab.
  • Click Unpack to decrypt and unpack the selected packages; the electronic documents from these packages will be placed in the archive of incoming documents on the Incoming tab.

Important. To decrypt data, the computer needs a certificate for which the package was encrypted by the sender, and which has access to the private digital signature key.
For automatic decryption, it is recommended to use a separate digital signature certificate for encryption and to enable the Remember password for certificate option.

Storage of received electronic documents

Received and unpacked electronic documents get to archive of incoming electronic documents. Click Electronic document archive to open the archive form.
