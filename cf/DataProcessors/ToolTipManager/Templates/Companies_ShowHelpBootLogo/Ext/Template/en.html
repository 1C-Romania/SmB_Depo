"How to load a logo?  "

It is very simple. If a logo:

- is already attached to the Organization: You need to click on the empty picture or in the field "Logo"( select )and select previously loaded file in a configuration.

- isn't attached yet to the Organization: You need to click on the empty picture, then in the opened choice form to click the Add command ( Add ) and specify the picture with a logo on your computer, and after that follow instructions from point 1.
