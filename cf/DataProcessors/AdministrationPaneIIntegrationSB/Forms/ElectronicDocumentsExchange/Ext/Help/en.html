Electronic document exchange

On this panel you can enable and configure the exchange of electronic documents between companies and counterparties, as well as direct communication with banks.

You can select the Exchange of electronic documents check box to use the electronic documents mechanism in the accounting system and configure other exchange parameters, for some options the use of digital signatures is mandatory.
Digital signatures enable the option to work with cryptography in the accounting system. To use data encryption and digital signatures, you need to have specialized crypto software installed on your PC, for example, Crypto CSP, ViPNet CSP or Microsoft CSP. You can configure crypto parameters on the Crypto settings form.
If you select the Electronic documents direct exchange with banks check box, it enables you to use direct interaction with the bank's software without installing the Bank client module on the user's PC. Direct exchange with the banks requires mandatory use of the Digital signatures.
The Agreements on the use of electronic documents are used to configure the parameters of a direct exchange with counterparties, exchange through the EDF operator, direct exchange with banks. You can use the Typical agreements on the use of electronic documents to customize those templates that will be used for quick fill out of agreements on direct exchange with counterparties.
If the Electronic documents delayed sending check box is not selected, the application transfers the package ready for sending to a recipient as soon as it is ready. If the check box is selected, the application accumulates the data for sending, forming a queue, and then sends packages according to the Send and receive electronic documents user command, or according to configured scheduled job.
