How is it possible to reduce the Amount of data Saved on the electronic documents exchange without a harm of their integrity?

When sending and receiving the electronic documents are transmitted in the files of transport packets that can be deleted if necessary.
For this in the "Archive of electronic documents" on the tab "All Packets" call the command "All actions" - "Removal of ED processed packets"
In the list of processed packets go the outgoing packets that have already been delivered to the recipient as well as incoming packets that have been completely unpacked and all the necessary information has been extracted from them.
