Accounts Payable

Accounts payable are controlled in the following way:

  • in the end of the analyzed period, a balance table for the Accounts payable register is generated
  • the obtained table is analysed and the situation is considered to be incorrect if a counterparty for one and the same contract and for one and the same order has both a balance of debts and a balance of advance payments

      By clicking the Execute setting-off of advances hyperlink, an automatic correction is initiated. At that the following occurs:
      • from the register of settlements, the application picks out documents in which settlement statuses were changed during the current period
      • in each selected document, the application fills clarification of payment for documents forming advances and prepayments for documents forming debts

      For documents of the "Initial balance entering" type, automatic correction is not provided for, i.e. if for one and the same contract both balance of advance payments and balance of debts are entered, the situation will be deemed incorrect. This situation will not be processed automatically by clicking the Execute setting-off of advances hyperlink.

      Accounts receivable

      Accounts receivable are controlled in the following way:

      • in the end of the analyzed period, a balance table for the Accounts receivable register is generated
      • the obtained table is analysed, and the situation is considered to be incorrect if a counterparty for one and the same contract and for one and the same order has both a balance of debt and a balance of advance payments

      By clicking the Execute setting-off of advances hyperlink, automatic correction is initiated. At that the following occurs:

      • from the register of settlements the application picks out documents in which settlements satuses were changed during the current period
      • in each selected document, the application fills clarification of payment for documents forming advances and prepayments for documents forming debts

      For documents of "Initial balance entering" type, the automatic correction is not provided for, i.e. if for one and the same contract balance of advance payments and balance of debts are entered, the situation will be deemed incorrect. this situation will not be processed automatically by clicking the Execute setting-off of advances hyperlink.

      Exchange Rate Differences

      The application checks flows related to rate difference accruals for the reported period.

      In case inadequately high exchange rate differences are found, the related message will be displayed to the user.

      Products and Services without Specifications

      The application ensures indication of a default specification for each products and services item which has one or more specifications.

      In case any defects are found, the user may:

      • in case for a specific products and services item only one specification was created – set this specification as the specification by default
      • in case there are more than one specification – choose one required from the list

      Results of corrections may influence results of analyses of subordinated sections. Influence of this section on the related subordinated sections will be described individually for every subordinated section.

      Subcontractors Reports without Specifications

      The application ensures that product specifications are indicated for all documents of the "Subcontractor report" type posted for the analysed period.

      In case blank specifications are found, the application will display only the documents for products of which default specifications are indicated. Therefore it is important to run all corrections retatively to the Products and services without specifications section. IN the displayed documents the user is proposed to define the specification and to rewrite the documents – this operation is made by clicking the Set specification hyperlink.

      Results of corrections may influence results of analyses of subordinated sections. Influence of this section on the related subordinated sections will be described individually for every subordinated section.

      Subcontractors Reports - Discrepancies Between Write-offs and Specifications

      The application ensures that products and services content and quantities in the "Inventory" tabular part in all documents of the "Subcontractor report" type posted for the analyzed period  correspond to the indicated specification. The application will check only documents having specifications indicated for their products, therefore making corrections for the Subcontractors reports without specifications section is important to ensure the highest quality of analysis for this section.

      When inconsistencies are found, the application asks the user to refill and repost each document separately by clicking the Refill and repost hyperlink.

      Production Documents without Specifications

      The application ensures that specifications are indicated in lines of the "Products" tabular section in all documents of the "Production" type posted for the analyzed period. IN case blank specifications are found, the application will display only those documents for products of which default specifications are indicated, therefore it is important to make all corrections for the Products and services without specifications section. For displayed documents the user is proposed to indicate the specification and rewrite the documents – this operation is made by clicking the Set specification hyperlink.

      Results of corrections may influence results of analyses of subordinated sections. Influence of this section on the related subordinated sections will be described individually for every subordinated section.

      Production Documents - Discrepancies Between Write-offs and Specifications

      The application ensures that the products and services content and quantities of the tabular section  in all documents of the "Production" type posted for the analyzed period correspond to the specifications indicated in the Production tabular section. The application will check only those documents in which for all products specifications are indicated, therefore making corrections for the  Production documents without specifications section is important to ensure the highest quality of analysis for this section.

      When inconsistencies are found, the user is proposed to refill and repost each document separately by clicking the Refill and repost hyperlink.

      Analysis of Purchase Prices

      During the analysis, the application will compare procurement prices in supplier invoices for the analyzed period with the previous supplies (supplier invoices for the previous period). The application will check only supplies of products and services of the "Inventory" type.

      In case of price deviations 5 times or more exceeding normal levels, the application will display the list of "problematic" products and services items to the user.

      This section does not provide for any automatic corrections, but for every item it is possible to view the report showing dynamics of price changes by clicking the Analysis result hyperlink.

      Control of Companies and Contracts in Documents

      During the analysis, the application checks matching of contracts and companies in documents posted for the current period with contracts and companies in the subordinated documents.

      In this context, subordinated are the documents referenced in fields of other documents.

      For example:

      • if a customer invoice is entered on the basis of a customer order, then the order is specified in the "Basis" field of this invoice and the order is deemed to be subordinated to the customer invoice;
      • when a supplier invoice was entered, 2 supplier orders were indicated in the "Inventory" table field containing 2 lines – in this case both orders will be deemed to be subordinated to the supplier invoice.

      In addition if accounting exchange is selected, the application will check matching of companies in the document and in the contract selected in this document.

      In case inconsistencies are found, the application will display to the user the corresponding report with the list of documents and incorrect situations.

      Banking Flow Accounts

      During the analysis, the application checks correspondence of cash flows (payments and cash receipts) to the predefined groups ("Payments", "Receipts") of cash flow accounts for the analyzed period.

      The check procedure is as follows:

      • if any cash flow account is included in the Payments predefined group, then it is assumed that for this account only cash outflows are possible. A return of money by a supplier is considered to be an exception (the Payments to suppliers cash flow account and the relevant types of operations in the Cash receipts and/or Payment receipt documents) – in case the conditions described are not fulfilled, a message will be displayed to the user indicating a possible accounting error;
      • if any cash flow account is included into the Receipts predefined group, then it is assumed that only inflows are possible for this account. A return of money to a customer is considered to be an exception (the Payments from customers cash flow account and corresponding types of operations in the Cash payment and/or Payment expense documents) – in case these conditions are not fulfilled, a message will be displayed to the user indicating a possible accounting error;
      • if a cash flow account is not included into either of the above listed predefined cash flow account groups, then its is assumed that for this account the amount of inflows shall be equal to the amount of outflows – in case this condition is not met, a message will be displayed to the user indicating a possible accounting error.

      You will be able to view the list of cash flow documents for the analyzed period for every cash flow account with detected accounting inaccuracies. The list of documents will be displayed as a separate report, which can be run by clicking on the specific account.
