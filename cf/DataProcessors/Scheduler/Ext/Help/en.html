Scheduler

Schedule chart allows you to analyze the schedule of work, services rendered, output of finished and half finished products for specified period (day, week, month).

Schedule chart is generated by work orders and/or production orders (if for the records of works and services performed used customer order, then for placing them into the schedule chart the order for production will be used). Appearance  options set in the settings form of Scheduler, called by click on button Setting on the right side of the command bar.

In a mode Resources workload you can quickly respond to the question of the load of a particular resource and plan it's workload. 

For this purpose, for example, you can call a special form of the document Work order or the document tools order, by double-clicking on the calendar on the line with the needed time in the field of selected resource.

User can schedule performinf works, services, production on the tab Work shedule or Resources worload, with documents Work order or tools order. The user sets status of the order In process and on the tab Additional indicates scheduled start and end dates of work in the fields Start and Finish.
