In this dialog box, you can define a period for summary calculation for the savings or accounting registers.

By default, the following dates are provided for limits of the calculated totals setup:

  • For savings registers - end of the previous month
  • For accounting registers - end of the current month

Enter data manually or with the button.

After the change, the date is automatically converted to the last day of the month of the selected date.

If the command of the period of the calculated totals setup is selected for one register, you can change only one parameter corresponding to the type of register.
