Manager dashboards

You can run the Manager’s monitor by clicking the relevant hyperlink on the navigation panel in the Analysis section. The Monitor includes the items as follows:

  • Cash. This widget shows the cash balance by the end of the specified day (EOD) for all the company’s petty cashes and current accounts. The diagrams show the balance dynamics over the last month. Click More to go to the Cash log.
  • Revenues and Expenses. This widget shows the total amount of the actual revenues, expenses and the resulting profit over the last month. The diagram shows the day-by-day profit/loss dynamics diagram over the last month. Click More to go to the Revenues/expenses log.
  • Orders. This widget shows the number of active outgoing and incoming orders, including orders as of the specified date. It also shows the number of overdue orders. Click More to go to Sales orders analysis and Purchase orders.
  • Sales. This widget shows the company’s sales amount and the number of goods and services sold over the last month. The diagram shows the day-by-day sales dynamics over the month. Click More to go to the Sales log.
  • Debtors. This is a list of the counterparties indebted to the company. The title shows the total number of the debtors and the total debt amount (including overdue debts). Five debtors with the greatest debts are shown in a separate list. The Advance payment column indicates the received advance payment not related to the order documents. Click More to go to the Accounts receivable report.
  • Creditors. This is a list of the counterparties to which the company owes money. The title shows the total number of the creditors and the total debt amount (including overdue debts). Five creditors with the greatest credits are shown in a separate list. The Advance payment column indicates the paid advance payment not related to the order documents. Click More to go to the Accounts payable report.


Using the selection fields on the command bar, the manager can find the information as of any date and about any company. The manager may also change, at his/her discretion, the mutual arrangement of the monitor widgets and fields, or disable the indication of unnecessary indicators (by means of the setup form available on the All actions – Edit form menu).
