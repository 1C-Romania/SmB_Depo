Key performance indicators

You can open the Key performance indicators form by clicking the hyperlink with the same name in the Analysis section navigation panel.

Charts and values of 5 indicators are presented on the form: "Revenue", "Cost", "Gross profit", "Expenses", and "Profit" by the month for the last year.

Indicators calculation formula:

  • Gross profit = Revenue - Cost;
  • Profit = Gross profit - Expenses.

There is an option to display information by any date and by any company on the form. You may change the relative positioning of the widgets or to limit the output of unnecessary indicators by using the settings form which is run from the All actions — Change form menu.
