Cash assets

To open the Cash assets form, in the navigation bar, in the Analysis section, click the corresponding hyperlink. You can see the following widgets on the form:

  • Banking balance. The widget shows cash balance in all cash registers and operating accounts of the company by the end of the day you specify. The chart shows dynamics of cash balance change over a period depending on the interval you set. To view the Statement of cash flows report, click More.
  • Banking inflow. The widget shows a total Amount of cash receipts associated with relevant accounts. The chart shows dynamics of cash inflow change over a period depending on the interval you set. To view the Bankingflow report, click More.
  • Banking outflow. The widget shows a total Amount of expenses associated with relevant accounts. The chart shows dynamics of expense change over a period depending on the interval you set. To view the Bankingflow report, click More.

Depending on the interval you set, the analyzed period is changed:

  • If the period is Day or Week, the last month is analyzed.
  • If the period is Month or Quarter, the last year is analyzed.

On the form, you can categorize information by any date with the interval you set or by any company. You can also change location of the widgets or hide the indicators you do not need by using the configuration form. To open the form, click All actions, and then click Edit form.
