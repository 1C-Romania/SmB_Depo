Waybill print

The data processor prints the Customer invoice document as a print form of the Customer invoice.

The form is printed from Customer invoice document when the "Print" button is pressed and the 1-T (Customer invoice) menu item is picked. Also a dialog window is opened, where it is required to fill an additional attributes that are needed for printing of the Customer invoice.

It is required to press the "Print Customer invoice" button after all required data has been filled.
