Inventory by CCD report

With the report, you can analyze company inventory by CCD. IN the Sheet mode, the report allows you to get complete information about the receipt, shipment and the current quantity of any import product to which CCD number is assigned. The report data is provided in the quantitative expression.
 
Information on receipt and expenses is not displayed in the Balances mode, only closing balances are shown.
