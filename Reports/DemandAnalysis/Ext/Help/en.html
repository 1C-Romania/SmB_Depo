Demand analysis report

The report allows to reveal the demand for raw materials and the materials necessary for production (both from own, and from supplied raw materials) and to pass to processing.
 

The report shows the demand for item and the sources of its supply, showing not closed demand.

The item demand can be supplied with the following methods:

  • reservation in storing place,
  • placement in the purchase order,
placement in the assembling and production order.
