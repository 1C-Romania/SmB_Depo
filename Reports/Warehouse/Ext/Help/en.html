Warehouse report

The report provides information about receipt, shipment and actual quantity of ProductsAndServices items at specified storage place. The report shows stock status, information for the warehouse Head. The data is grouped by warehouses.

The report contains information about opening and closing balance at company warehouses of the company.The report data are shown in quantitative and accrual expression.
If the order model is used, it is possible to see information about quantity of ProductsAndServices items that has to be WrittenOff by Goods expenses or that has to be received by Supplier invoices.

The report may be generated in two modes – Statement and Balances. IN the Balances mode the report does not output information about receipt and expense, just closing balance of ProductsAndServices items at selected .
