Income and expenses report

Use this report to show the company's revenue, expenses and profit for the specified period and the dynamics of the values calculated with accrual method (on realization basis).
 
The report contains variants where data is grouped by business activities or customer orders.

In the dynamics report variant, the periodicity is defined in the settings. By default, it is automatically defined according to the selected report period. For example, when you create a monthly report, the periodicity is Day; for an annual report, it is Month.
