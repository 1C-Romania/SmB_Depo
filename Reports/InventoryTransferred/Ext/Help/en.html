Inventory transferred report

With the report, you can get complete information about inventory transferred to the commission, processing and safe custody.

Information about opening and closing inventory balances of counterparties is displayed in the report. The report data is presented in quantity and totals.
 
The report can be generated in two modes – Statement and Balances. IN the Balances mode the data on receipts and expenses is not shown in the report, you can see the final balances only.
