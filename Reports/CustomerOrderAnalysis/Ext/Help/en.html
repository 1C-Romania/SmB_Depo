Customer order analysis report

The report allows to analyze the content and how the customer order is executed regarding shipment and payment.

Regarding shipment by order such indicators are shown:

  • Ordered – number of goods specified in the customer's order.
  • Shipped - number of goods shipped by order.
  • Left to ship – total number of goods left to be shipped by order.
  • Reserved at warehouse – number of goods which are reserved in a warehouse by order.
  • Placed in orders – COUNT of goods which is placed in orders to suppliers.
  • Balance available - COUNT of goods registered at warehouses and can be used for order supply.
  • Left to supply – COUNT of goods which should be additionally reserved or placed in orders to execute the order. When calculating this COUNT the available balance of goods at warehouse isn't considered - it is supposed that the user decides himself on the use of the available warehouse balance.

Regarding payment of goods by order the following indicators are shown:

  • Document Amount – the Amount which is specified in the customer's order.
  • Planned - the Amount planned by order in the order itself on a tab the Payments calendar and documents of the cash flow planning.
  • Prepayment - the prepayment Amount by order.
  • Payments - the payment Amount by order.
  • Total - total Amount of prepayment and payment by order.
  • Left to pay – the Amount needed to be repaid, to pay for order in full.
