Plan/actual analysis of production, work performance and service rendering report

With the report, you can analyze a ratio of target and actual output, performed works, and rendered services.

The report shows difference between a quantity and an amount of planned production and produced goods indicating percentages of deviation, compared with the target data.

Target data is generated using work orders - by works and services, and also with production orders - by inventory, works and services.

Actual data is generated using work orders, customer invoices, acceptance certificates and production documents.
