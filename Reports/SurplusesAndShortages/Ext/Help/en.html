Surpluses and shortages report

The report allows you to get the information on surpluses and shortages of inventory registered by the Inventory receipt and Inventory write off documents, and also a total difference between surpluses and shortages.

The report data is presented by amount and total amount.

If the data is filtered by the inventory document, the data of the Inventory receipt and Inventory write off documents for which the inventory document is specified as a basis, will be selected for the report.
