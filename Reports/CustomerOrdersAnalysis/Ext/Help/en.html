Customer order analysis report

 

The report allows to analyze the content and the course of customer orders execution.
 
The report is grouped by orders. For each order the lacking (not provided with sources) COUNT of item positions is calculated. Thus, with the help of the report it is possible to define the demand for purchase or manufacture of this or that item position necessary for order execution.

The item demand can be satisfied by the following methods:

  • reservation at warehouse,
  • placement in the purchase order,
  • placement in the assembling and production order.


The fact of reservation and placement is registered with documents based on the Customer Order document. The COUNT of the positions reserved in a warehouse is also is displayed in the report.
