Products and services batches catalog

This catalog stores information about inventory batches and batches accounting settings.

The catalog is used if the Accounting by batches system option is selected. The option value is defined in the Settings - Accounting settings section.

For products and services received from external counterparties, batching accounting is required. There should be at least one batch with the Goods on commission status for the goods accepted on commission. Respectively: for materials accepted for processing - Supplied raw materials for processing, for inventory accepted for safe custody - Safe custody.
