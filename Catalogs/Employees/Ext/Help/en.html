Employees catalog

The information about employees of the Company is Saved in this list.

The the same person (or individual) can be considered as several accounting objects. For example, some person may work sometime in an company and quit; and later this person can be hired again. IN this case, two employees, hired in different time periods and, in common case, to the different working places, correspond to the same individual. Thus, IS two closely related accounting objects in the personnel accounting: employees and individuals.

The information editing is done in a separate window, which is opened from the list form.

The following information describing an employee is entered:

  • Occupation Type. Main Occupation or Jobsharing.
  • Individual. The individual that corresponds to the current employee, selected from the Individuals list.
  • GL Accounts. Belongs to group of the attributes:
    • With Personnel. The GL account of personnel accounts is specified in this field, selected from the Managerial chart of accounts.
    • With Advance Holder. The GL account of advance holder accounts is specified in this field, selected from the Managerial chart of accounts.
    • By Overrun. The GL account by overrun is specified in this field, selected from the Managerial chart of accounts.

One of the options should be selected when new item is Creatingd:

  • Create New Performer and Enter Personal Data into Individuals Catalog. In this case, both the Performers and the Individuals items will be Creatingd and linked between each other.
  • Create New Performer by Selecting it from Individuals Catalog. In this case, the individual is available for selection; the employee description is filled when individual is picked and link with the personal data is Creatingd.
  • Create New Performer. A new item of the Performer List is Creatingd.

The EmploymentContract document will be Creatingd if in the process of creating a new employee all the fields underlined in red are filled in on the tab EmploymentContract Information.

Since the item is Creating, personal and employment information are not available for editing anymore. This information can be modified via the Individuals list and the EmploymentContract document.
