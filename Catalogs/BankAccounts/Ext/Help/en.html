Bank accounts catalog

Bank accounts of all companies and individuals (own and external) are stored in the Bank accounts catalog.

The Bank accounts catalog is subordinate to the Companies, Counterparties and Individuals catalogs.

The bank account list of a company, subordinate organization, or individual depends on the bank accounts subordinate to the corresponding item of the Companies, Counterparties or Individuals catalog.

You can select a default bank account for each counterparty or subordinate organization. This account will be automatically entered into the payment documents.

Analytical accounting of cash assets is provided for each bank account.

To edit bank account information, use a separate window opened from the Bank accounts list form.

Enter the following bank account information:

  • Account kind. It is selected from the following list: settlement, deposit, loan, etc.
  • Account number. Bank account number. 
  • Currency. The account currency. Currency is selected from the Currencies catalog.
  • Bank of bank account. A group of fields:
    • BIC. Russian Central Bank Identifier.
    • Corr account. Correspondent account.
    • Bank. Bank, where the account is opened.
    • City. Bank city.
  • Indirect payments. Set the option Indirect Payments if bank, where the account is opened, uses a correspondent account in another bank for transfers (Bank for Indirect Payments).
  • Bank for indirect payments. Belongs to a group of the attributes:
    • BIN. Bank Identification Number.
    • Corr account. Correspondent account.
    • Bank. The bank, where the account is opened.
    • City. Bank city.
  • Payment order print settings. A group of fields:
    • Edit organization name. Defines editability of the organization's name text. A similar algorithm is used for counterparty accounts.
    • Company name in "Payer" field. The text to be inserted into the Payer field in the payment documents. A similar algorithm is used for counterparty accounts.
    • KPP indication variant. Defines how to state KPP indication: either in all payment documents, or only in tax transfer. A similar algorithm is used for counterparty accounts.
    • Month indication variant. Defines the month output: either a string or a number.
    • Integer dollar sum amounts showed without cents. If the check box is selected, the payment document amount will be indicated without cents with a "=" sign. Otherwise, a "-" sign will be used. For example, 20 dollars will be printed as 40= and 40-00 respectively.
  • Additional information. A group of fields:
    • Opening date. Date when the bank account was opened.
    • Closing date. Date when the bank account was closed.
    • Permission number and date. Number and date of the Central bank permission for opening a currency account.
    • Bank account navigation panel 
    • GL Account. It is selected from the Managerial chart of accounts. Required for bank accounts of organizations. For counterparties and individuals, the field is optional.

It is enough to fill the BIC or the Corr account field to select a bank. Enter your value into one of the fields to find the bank in the catalog and automatically fill out the remaining fields. If there is no bank with such BIC or Corr account in the bank list, you can select a bank from the bank list manually or enter a new bank.
