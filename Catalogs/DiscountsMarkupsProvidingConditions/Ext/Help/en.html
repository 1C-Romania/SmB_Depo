Discount condition (markups, limits) catalog

With 1C:Small Business, you can get a discount in the following cases:

· Single sale - for sales within one document.

· Kit purchase - for particular goods bought at a time.

Discount condition name is created automatically based on the data you entered.

For single sale

In the list of discount conditions, click For single sale and fill out conditions of sales volume calculation. The discount can be calculated by Sum or Quantity of the document goods items with the following limits of the number you entered: "<" (less than), ">" (greater than), "<=" (less than or equal) or ">=" (greater than or equal).

If particular products and services or their groups are specified, then the conditions are checked only by them.

Condition name is created automatically based on the data you entered.

For kit purchase

You can specify a list of goods that have to be bought at a time as a discount condition. For example if you buy garden tools (4 shovels and rakes), you will get a 50% discount on a clipper.

Select the discount condition value For kit purchase, add Products and services to the list, and then specify Measurement unit and Quantity of each kit item.
