Key resources catalog

The catalog is designed for saving the information about resources of company, used for executing of job-tickets and production orders.

Resource may belong to one or some resource types. Every resource always belongs to special resource type – All resources.

The resource may be connected to employee, brigade or may be connected to nothing.

The Power IsSet for the resource – the value of resource availability on the time interval. By default, the power equals 1. It means that if the work of these resources is planned on certain interval, than it will be absolutely busy on this interval.

The Schedule IsSet for the resource. The schedule may be pointed from the existing schedules. With buttons Working hours and Non working hours the rejections from schedule are entering.
