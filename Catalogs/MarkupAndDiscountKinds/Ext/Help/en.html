Markup and discount kinds catalog

The information about markup and discount types is Saved in this list. For example: cash payment, sale, etc. The discount (markup) value is specified in percent.

A discount is entered with the plus sign, and а markup – with minus sign.

 

The information editing is done in a separate window, which is opened from the list form.

The following information is entered:

  • Percent. Discount, markup value presented in percent.
  • Note. Any arbitrary comment.
