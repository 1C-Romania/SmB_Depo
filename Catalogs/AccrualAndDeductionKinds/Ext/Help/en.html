Accrual and deduction kinds catalog

The catalog contains a list of all employee accrual and deduction information required for payroll.

 

You can group kinds of accruals and deductions according to the catalog classifier groups, e.g. "Accrual" and "Deduction".

You can edit the accrual and deduction information in a separate dialog box opened in the catalog list form.

 The following information is to be specified:

  • Type. Either accrual or deduction.
  • GL account. The default GL account for accrued payroll.
  • Tax kind. Tax kind used for the tax accrual.
  • Formula. Formula used to calculate amounts of accruals and deductions. The formula is edited in a separate dialog box. The formula may include the elements of the Calculation parameters catalog specified at the bottom of the form, numeric characters, characters (+, -, *, /) and functions (? (), Min (), Max (), Round (), etc.) Select the calculations parameter by double-clicking it. To view and edit parameters, click Edit (or open) current item on the command bar or press F4.
