Petty cashes catalog

The information about petty cashes of own Companies is Saved in this list.

 

The information editing is done in a separate window, which is opened from the list form.

The following information is entered:

  • Currency by default. The currency that is automatically filled in the documents. The currency is selected from the Currencies list.
  • GL Account. The account is selected from the Managerial chart of accounts. This is a required field.
