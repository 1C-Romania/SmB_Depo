Counterparty contracts catalog

The catalog stores counterparty contracts. It is subordinate to the Counterparties catalog.

 

The contract list with a counterparty depends on the contracts subordinate to the relevant item of the Counterparties catalog item. 

 

You can specify a default contract for each counterparty; this contract will be automatically set in all documents.

Analytical accounting of settlements with the counterparties is performed for each contract.

You can edit contract information in a separate window opened from the form of the counterparty contract catalog list.

Specific contract information includes:

  • Contract number is specified.
  • From. Contract date.
  • Settlement currency for the contract with the counterparty (it is selected from the Currencies catalog).
  • Settlements in standard units. The check box for settlements in standard units.
  • Export sales means that the contract transactions suppose 0% VAT.
  • Organization. Internal organization concluding the contract. It is selected from the Organizations catalog.
     
  • Customer payment term. The payment delay for the current customer contract.
  • Supplier payment term. The payment delay for the current supplier contract.
  • Price kind, discount kind, counterparty price kind. The default values are entered into documents automatically when you select the contract.
  • Contract kind for counterparty (supplier, customer, consignor, consignee or other). An optional field used only for data exchange with the Enterprise accounting configuration.
