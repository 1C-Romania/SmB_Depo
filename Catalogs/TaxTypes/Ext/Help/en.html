Tax kinds catalog

The information about tax types and associated GL accounts is Saved in this list: VAT, corporate tax and other. It is used for analytical accounting of accounts with the government. Tax accrual is performed by the Taxes Accrual document.

The following information is entered:

  • GL Account. The GL Account of the accrued tax.
  • Reimbursement account. The GL Account of the tax for reimbursement.
