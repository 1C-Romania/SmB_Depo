Business activities catalog

Income and expenses are accumulated by the business activities defined in this list. The company's financial indicators are Saved by the business activities as well.

 

The information editing is done in a separate window, which is opened from the catalog list form.

The following information describing a contract is defined here:

  • Income and Expense GL Accounts. Belongs to group of the attributes:
    • Revenue from sales. Revenue from sales GL account.
    • Cost of Goods Sold. Cost of Goods Sold GL account.
    • Profit. Profit GL account.
