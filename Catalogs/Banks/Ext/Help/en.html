Banks catalog

The information about banks, where both own Companies and Counteparties have Bank accounts.

 The catalog for the entering and editing is called from the following section: "Enterprise" / Catalogs / "Banks".

The list of banks can be filled in automatically using the Russian Banks Classifier. To fill in use the Add banks from the Russian banks classifier button.

The catalog can be also updated from the disk of "Infornation Technology Support" or from the "RusBusinessConsulting" website.

The list has a multilevel hierarchical structure. The items can be regrouped by moving items from one group to another.

In the form of the list there is a command "Update banks from the classifier". This command updates the previously selected (added from the classifier) banks. If for the item of the bank is selected manual Change, then the update is not going to be performed.

The following information is Saved in this catalog:

  • Abbreviation. Abbreviation of the bank, where a company's or a company's bank account is opened.
  • BIN. Bank identification number.
  • Corr account. Correspondent account.
  • City. Data about bank location.
  • Address. Bank postal code and a mailing address.
  • Phone Numbers. Phone numbers of the bank.
