Specifications catalog

The information about the specifications of finished goods production, jobs execution, and services offering is Saved in this list.

The information editing is done in a separate window, which is opened from the list form.

The following information describing a specification is entered:

  • Characteristic. Should be specified in case, when specification describes finished goods production variant by the specific characteristic.
  • Content. The tabular section:
    • Row Type. Type of line of goods content. It can be equal to one of the following values:
      • Material. The raw material, which is purchased from the external vendors.
      • Assembly. The assembled unit, half-finished product, produced by the enterprise.
      • Node. Not existing unit, a set, containing several items.
      • Expense. A service of the third party companies of production nature.
    • ProductsAndServices. The ProductsAndServices item which is contained in the finished goods structure, according to the specified type of the content line.
    • Characteristic. Characteristic of the ProductsAndServices  item.
    • UOM. ProductsAndServices item unit of measure.
    • Quantity. The finished product manufacturing expense norm of the ProductsAndServices item.
    • Quantity of Finished Goods. The COUNT of finished goods, for which item expense norms are specified.
  • Operations. The tabular section:
    • Operation. The technological operation of the production process.
    • Time Norm. The time norm for the operation execution (hours).
    • Quantity of Finished Goods. The COUNT of finished goods, for which operation execution time norms are specified.
