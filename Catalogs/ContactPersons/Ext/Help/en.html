Contact persons catalog

The contact persons through which communication between the enterprise and a company occurs are Saved in this list, which is subordinated to the Counterparties list.

 

The list of the contact persons is limited to those persons, which are subordinated to the respective item of the Counterparties list.

The information editing is done in a separate window, which is opened from the list form.

The following information describing a contract person is entered:

  • Presentation. The contact person's last, middle and first name.
  • Individual. Selected from the Individuals list.
  • Responsible. The information about the employee who is responsible for the current item entry.
  • Contact Registration Company contract date. The initial contact registration date or the date of the first contact.
  • Contact Stop Company contract date. The contact termination registration date or the contact termination date.

The contact person roles are specified on the Contact person roles tab.

Notes of the employee about contacts with the customer are specified on the Notes tab.

The contact person contact information, such as 'email', 'phone number', is entered on the Addresses, PhoneNumbers tab.

The additional attributes, defined by user, are specified on the Additional Attributes tab.
