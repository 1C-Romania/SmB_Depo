How being on a workplace and working with CR Offline to view goods list loaded at CR?

For these purposes the command of the exchange list "View Goods List" is used.

 

What actions can be executed in the equipment goods list?

In the viewing form it is possible to set selection on goods: "All" "Changed" (set when opening form by default), "With Errors" (without the price and/or a barcode). As it is possible to export the changed goods in CR/scales, to clear and completely to reload CR/scales, to print the list of goods with the CR/PLU codes, to print price tags of exported goods.
