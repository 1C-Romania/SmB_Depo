"How to fill in the CCD numbers?". 

It's easy: 

1. Select the country of origin and the CCD number.

3. Mark the lines with ticks. For this above the table  use the command "Mark all" () or "Set the mark for lines with the same country" (see group of commands "Work with the country");

3. Press the "Install" button;

ATTENTION!!!

If it is necessary to clear the CCD number of the country of origin it is necessary to mark the lines and to perform the command "Install" by NOT filling in the fields "CCD number" and/or "Country of origin".
