It is intended for filtering data during Interactive data synchronization.

Filtering the data for import

  • The filter period is used for the documents. IN the For the period field, click Select, specify the period. The dates can be specified manually or choosen from a calendar. By clicking the Show standard periods link you can pick up the period quickly.
  • Click Add condition.
  • Select Field for filtering.
  • Select Comparison kind.
    • Comparison kinds Equal, Not equal, Belongs to group, Not in group assume a choice of one value. By means of the Equal or Belongs to group comparison kinds the application will select only those records that correspond to the specified value absolutely.
    • Comparison kinds In list, Belongs to group from list, Not in list, Not in group from list assume a choice of several values. Comparison kinds Belongs to group from list, Not in group from list are available if there are groups in the corresponding lists for selection of values.
    • By means of the In list or Belongs to group from list comparison kinds the items that contain a certain value listed in the list will be selected.
    • Comparison kinds Not equal, Not in group, Not in list, Not in group from list are the opposite types of the corresponding comparison kinds>. As a result of applying these types of comparison all items will be selected except the elements that are specified.
    • For the Filled and Not filled c>omparison kinds the selection of value is not required. By means of the Filled comparison kind, for example, you can select the documents in which the goods price is missing.
  • Specify Value.
    • Depending on a comparison kind the value can be written manually or choosen from the list.
    • By means of the More - Use as field value command a field of the document or item of the list can be specified as a value. Select the field from the list suggested.

Conditions grouping

  • If neccessary, there may be several conditions for filtering, in this case they are grouped by means of the logical operations AND, OR, NOT by clicking the Group conditions button. Select the conditions for grouping, and then click the button. IN this case all chosen conditions will be included into the group.
  • To cancel the grouping of conditions, select them, and then run the More - Ungroup command.
  • The conditions for groups can be created by means of the More - Add new folder command, and then by dragging the conditions you need, or by creating new conditions in the group.
