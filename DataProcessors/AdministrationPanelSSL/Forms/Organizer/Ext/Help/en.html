In this panel is performed for the reminder settings.

  • Email system account setup
    		•  Setup of the main email account for sending the notifications out of the application.
  • Email accounts
    		•  Administering of all the email accounts.

  • Reminder
    		•  Tick of the reminders adding which are displyed at the appointed time. Reminders may be arbitrary or depending on the object, referring for example to the item of the catalog, the document or other application object.

     

    See also: Application settings.
