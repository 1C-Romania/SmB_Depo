Accounts payable

To open the Accounts payable form, in the navigation bar, in the Analysis section, click the corresponding hyperlink. You can see the following widgets on the form:

  • Creditors. The widget shows a total amount of the company debts to its counterparties and advances that are not associated with any order records. You can use the chart to display the percentage of the total debt to every counterparty and a total debt to the counterparty. The chart is built for the date you specify. To create the Accounts payables by period report, click More.
  • Accounts payables by period. The widget shows a period of the debt to counterparties and a percentage of long-term debts (over a month) and relatively new debts that the company has got over the last week or month for the specified date. To create the Accounts payables by period (currency man.) report, click More.
  • Debts dynamics and creditors with overdue debts. The chart represents dynamics of debt changes by days over the last month. IN the Creditors with overdue debts widget, you can see overdue accounts payables for the specified date. To create the Accounts payables by period (currency of management accounting) report, click More.

You can categorize information by any date or company in the form. You can also change location of the widgets or hide the indicators you do not need by using the configuration form. To open the form, click All actions, and then click Edit form.
