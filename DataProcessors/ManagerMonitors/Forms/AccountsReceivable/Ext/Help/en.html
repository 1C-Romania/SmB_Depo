Accounts receivable

You can open the Accounts receivable form by clicking the relevant hyperlink on the navigation panel in the Analysis section. The form includes the items as follows:

  • Debtors. The widget shows total accounts receivable and the amount of advance payments not charged to the documents of orders. The diagram helps to estimate the percentage of receivables per counterparty against the total accounts receivable. The diagram is constructed for specified date. Click Read more to see the Accounts receivable report (manag. curr.).
  • Accounts receivable by date. This widget gives an overview of the receivables by date and percentage of old receivables (30 days old or more) and recent receivables (7 days or 30 days prior to specified date) against the total accounts receivable. Click Read more to see the Accounts receivable by date (manag. curr.) report.
  • Debt dynamics and overdue debtors. The diagram shows debt dynamics by days for the last month. The Overdue debts widget shows the information on accounts receivable that become overdue on the date specified. Click Read more to see the Accounts receivable by date (manag. curr.) report.

You can use the form to review data by any date and company. You can change the position of widgets or disable unnecessary indicators using the settings on the All actions – Change form pop-up menu.
