About the service of checking the counterparties of the Federal Tax Service

There is an option to check a counterparty using the Internet and FTS dedicated web service. The FTS Check Counterparty Details service allows to check online the existence of a counterparty by its key details, namely, TIN and KPP.

IMPORTANT!
Nowadays FTS service is functioning in the test mode.

Counterparties are checked using EGRN (Unified State Register of Taxpayers), the check result is one of the statuses as follows:

Contractor is in the FTS database - It means that the taxpayer is registered in EGRN with a status of the current taxpayer within ± 6 days interval from the date of the query
Activity terminated or KPP is changed - It means that the taxpayer is registered in EGRN but has no status of valid taxpayer within ± 6 day period from the query date.
You can consider this response as follows: either counterparty with the specified combination of TIN and KPP has ceased the activity, or the specified KPP was changed, i.e. the combination of TIN and KPP was previously valid, but now it is not valid.
KPP does not corresponds the FTS database - It means that the taxpayer KPP doesn't correspond to TIN specified in the query.
The message means that there is no such TIN and KPP combination in EGRN and has never been.
Counterparty is not in the FTS database - It means that the taxpayer with the specified TIN is not registered in EGRN

The check is started at entering a new counterparty and changing the details of the existing counterparty. Check status is shown in the counterparty's card, list forms and Counterparty catalog selection forms.

The result of the counterparty check is explicitly shown in the counterparty card.

In case of a negative result of the check, the TIN and KPP fields are colored grey in the list forms and Counterparty catalog selection.

If FTS informs of the KPP and TIN nonconformance, then TIN and KPP of such counterparty are colored red in the list form.

In addition to the check when editing a counterparty card, the application checks all counterparties from the catalog in the background once a week.

Counterparty check settings are managed in the Settings - Application settings - Counterparty check settings section. IN 1C:Company the check is enabled by default via the Internet service mode, and it does not require additional settings.
