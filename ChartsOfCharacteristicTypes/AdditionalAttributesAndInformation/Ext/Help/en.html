Additional attributes and data are used in property sets.

All additional attributes and data can be shown in reports and lists.

Select an additional property kind considering the following:

  • Additional attributes are an integral part of the "object with properties". They are stored in the object tabular section and are accessible to the users who have access to the "object with properties" itself. Additional attributes are edited in the object form along with its other attributes.
  • Additional data is used to store the object information that is not its integral part. Additional data is usually accessible for preview and/or edit by users who do not have access to the "object with properties". Additional data is edited in a separate form that can be opened from the window of the "object with properties".

When selecting a type of the Additional value or Additional value (hierarchy), you can specify a list of possible values or values with hierarchy of the additional attribute or data. To do it, click Create in the window of additional attribute or data.
