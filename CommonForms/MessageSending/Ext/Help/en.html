Designed to send an email message.

You can use the  Create message or Send buttons to send email messages from application objects, e.g. from the user window, etc.

Creating email

  • In the To field, you can enter multiple receiving email addresses separated by commas. An email address can be stated as follows:
    • smith@server - a short variant, an inbox address
    • Smith smith@server - a full email address, includes the addressee name and their email address
    • ivanov (the mail server must be able to match it with the full address).
  • Fill in the Subject field
  • Write your message
  • To attach extra files to your message, click Attach file.
    • To delete an attachment, select it and press Del.
  • An account for automatic email sending/receipt will be offered automatically.
  • You can use the Response address field to enter the email address for possible responses. Fill in this field if the incoming mail of the system account is not checked (e.g. if the account is designed only for sending).

Sending

  • After you finish your message, click Send.
