You can view the basic properties of a digital signature in this form.

Digital signature is an attribute of electronic document by which you can make ensure the invariance of an electronic document since its signature. The invariance of an electronic document is controlled by checking the compliance of an digital signature and document, as well as by checking the certificate's validity. You can run the invariance control of an electronic document at any moment.

With the help of digital signature you can:

  • Sign application objects (for example, the Name and Short description fields);
  • Sign content of files.

Signature data

  • Signature author - information on the owner of the certificate;
  • Signature established by - the application automatically displays the user who established the signature;
  • Signature date - date and time of the signature installation;
  • Comment - additional information on the signature.

Saving signature

  • Click Save to file, specify the folder on a network or local drive to save the digital signature on the computer.

Viewing certificate

  • Click Open certificate to view digital signature key certificate properties.
