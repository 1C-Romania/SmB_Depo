Structure of subordination of the document

The structure of subordination of the document displays as a tree. The call of this report is carried out of the forms of the relevant documents, on button "Hierarchy structure".

 

In the form of a generated report you can perform the following actions:

  • open a form of the current document in the list,
  • post a document,
  • perform undo posting of a document,
  • update list of documents,
  • generate a new tree relatively to the current document.

When forming the tree, list of documents to which current document is subordinated, in some cases the may not be fully displayed. This occurs when the documents themselves are subordinated and at the same time are subordinated to the same document. IN this case constructing of the structure to "up" is limited with those documents.
