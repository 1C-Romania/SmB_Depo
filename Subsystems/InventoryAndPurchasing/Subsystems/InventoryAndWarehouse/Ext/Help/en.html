Inventory and warehouse subsystem

The system is intended to do accounting of enterprise inventory on many warehouses.

In a configuration the following opportunities of the inventory accounting are provided:

 

  • the separate inventory accounting of  – own goods, materials, finished goods, and also the goods accepted and transferred to the commission, the materials accepted and transferred to processing,
  • accounting of the arbitrary item characteristics (color, size etc.), and also inventory batches ,
  • accounting by cells of storage locations (zones, racks, cells, etc.).
