Sales subsystem

In applied solution of "Small business" the following schemes of sales are supported:

  • Sales from warehouse and by the order,
  • shipment on credit or on an advance payment
  • commission trade,
  • consignment sales.


Goods, works, and services sales are recorded by entering Customer invoice, Services acceptance certificates. Document.CustomerInvoiceNote/Help">Sales Invoice can also be Creatingd on the basis of these documents.
The system provides the complete set of tools to generate the required printing forms.

The system allows you to:

  • register and maintain list of customers and contact information ,
  • register contracts with customers,
  • enter Customer order – in this case the order is the contract specification, in which list of goods, works, terms of delivery (completion) and prices are reflected,
  • enter work orders,
  • combine customer orders into projects,
  • Creating the shipping schedule,
  • analyze the demand for goods, works, services, formed by customer orders and fulfilling them with reservation of available residues of goods in warehouses or with posting a purchase orders and/or production orders,
  • track of fulfilling customer orders with goods, products, works and services.
