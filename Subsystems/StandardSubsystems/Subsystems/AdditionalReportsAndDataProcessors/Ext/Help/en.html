The configuration allows you to attachto the IB and use additional (external) reports and data processors in "1C:Enterprise". They allow you to extend the functionality of the configuration, without making any modifications to it.

With additional reports and data processors you are able :

  • From the object forms and list of objects:
    • generate reports, linked with object;
    • generate additional printing forms of object;
    • based on the parent objects Creating and populate child ("linked") objects;
    • fill attributes of tabular sections of objects.
  • From the command interface:
    • perform processing of arbitrary IB data;
    • generate arbitrary reports.
