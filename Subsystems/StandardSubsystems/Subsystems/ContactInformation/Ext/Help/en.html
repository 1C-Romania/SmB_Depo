Configuration allows to unoformly Save and process the the contact information for different objects. For example, the cataloges Individuals, Companies and Users provide the same opportunity to work with the addresses or the phone numbers.

Maintain the contact information involves presetting the types of the contact information which is recommended to be performed on the stage of the initial filling of the infobase. The setting is to be done in the Administration section by the command of Types of contact information on the navigation bar.
